## Skills and Strengths

### Defensive Positioning
Tchouameni is a master of positioning in the defensive midfield role. He reads the game exceptionally well, intercepting passes before they reach their target and shielding the back four with intelligence and discipline. His ability to be in the right place at the right time reduces the need for last-ditch tackling.

### Physical Presence
At 1.88 m with a powerful build, Tchouameni dominates physical battles in midfield. He is strong in the tackle, wins aerial duels consistently, and has the stamina to cover ground for 90 minutes. His physicality makes him an ideal shield in front of the defense.

### Passing Range
While primarily a defensive player, Tchouameni possesses an excellent passing range. He can play short, intricate passes to retain possession or launch long, diagonal balls to switch play. This ability makes him a crucial link between defense and attack in both the France and Real Madrid systems.

### Versatility
Tchouameni can play as a defensive midfielder, a box-to-box midfielder, or even as a centre-back when required. This versatility has been invaluable for Real Madrid, where he has filled in at center-back during injury crises. For France, he is the undisputed anchor of the midfield.

### Composure Under Pressure
Despite his age, Tchouameni plays with remarkable composure. He rarely panics under pressure, maintains possession in tight spaces, and makes calm decisions even in high-stakes matches.

## Funny and Interesting Facts

### 1. The Record-Breaking Transfer That Could Have Gone Wrong
When Real Madrid signed Tchouameni from Monaco in 2022 for a reported 80 million euros (plus add-ons), many questioned whether the fee was justified for a player with only two full seasons of top-flight experience. The deal was so expensive that even some Real Madrid fans were skeptical. But Tchouameni handled the pressure with remarkable maturity, earning a starting spot within weeks. What most people don't know is that Liverpool were also in advanced negotiations for him, and Tchouameni reportedly chose Real Madrid because he dreamed of playing in the Santiago Bernabeu since childhood. His agent later revealed that the Liverpool offer was actually higher in terms of personal salary, but Tchouameni took less money to fulfill his dream.

### 2. The Champions League First
Tchouameni went over 150 appearances for Real Madrid without scoring a Champions League goal -- a remarkable drought for a player of his quality. He had come close on numerous occasions, hitting the post, having goals disallowed, and watching saves deny him. When he finally scored his first Champions League goal in the 2025/26 season against Benfica, his celebration was one of pure relief and joy. Teammates mobbed him, with Vinicius Jr. reportedly shouting "finally!" as they celebrated. The goal went viral, and Tchouameni later posted on social media with the caption: "About time," accompanied by a laughing emoji.
