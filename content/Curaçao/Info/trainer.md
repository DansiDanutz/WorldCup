# Dick Advocaat — Head Coach

## Profile
- **Full Name:** Dirk Nicolaas "Dick" Advocaat
- **Date of Birth:** September 27, 1947
- **Place of Birth:** The Hague, Netherlands
- **Nationality:** Dutch
- **Appointed:** 2025

## Coaching Career
One of the most experienced coaches in world football, Advocaat has managed:
- Netherlands National Team (multiple stints)
- Rangers FC (Scotland) — won domestic treble
- Zenit St. Petersburg — won UEFA Cup
- Sunderland, Fulham (Premier League)
- Serbia, South Korea national teams
- Feyenoord, PSV Eindhoven

## Tactical Philosophy
- **Pragmatic:** Adapts tactics to the quality of players available
- **Defensive Organization:** Well-structured defensive units
- **Counter-Attacking:** Uses pace on the break against stronger teams
- **Experience:** Vast knowledge of tournament football
