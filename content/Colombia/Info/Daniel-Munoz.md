### Skills and Strengths

- **Engine**: Incredible stamina — covers the entire right flank for 90 minutes without losing intensity
- **Defensive solidity**: Strong in one-on-one defending, reads the game well
- **Attacking threat**: Overlaps constantly, delivers quality crosses into the box
- **Tactical intelligence**: Knows when to bomb forward and when to hold position
- **Versatility**: Can play as a wing-back, full-back, or even as a wide midfielder
