## Team History

### The Cafeteros' World Cup Journey

Colombia is one of South America's most passionate football nations, with a World Cup history marked by brilliance, heartbreak, and resurgence. The team has appeared in **seven World Cups** (1962, 1990, 1994, 1998, 2014, 2018, 2026) and is renowned for its attacking flair and technical quality.

### World Cup Highlights

| Year | Host | Result | Key Moment |
|------|------|--------|------------|
| 1990 | Italy | Round of 16 | First knockout stage appearance; Carlos Valderrama's golden generation emerged |
| 1994 | USA | Group Stage | Entered as favorites after stunning Argentina 5-0 in qualifying; suffered tragic elimination |
| 2014 | Brazil | Quarterfinals | James Rodriguez won the Golden Boot (6 goals); stunning volley vs Uruguay voted goal of the tournament |
| 2018 | Russia | Round of 16 | Knocked out by England on penalties despite a valiant fight |

### The Resurgence

- **16-year absence**: Colombia failed to qualify for 2002, 2006, and 2010 World Cups
- **2014 renaissance**: James Rodriguez's breakout tournament reignited national passion
- **2024 Copa America**: Reached the final under Nestor Lorenzo, losing 1-0 to Argentina in extra time

## Playing Style

Under coach **Nestor Lorenzo**, Colombia plays an organized 4-3-3 that balances defensive solidity with explosive counter-attacking:

- **Pace on the wings** — Luis Diaz and Luis Sinisterra provide devastating width
- **Creative midfield** — James Rodriguez orchestrates with vision and set-piece quality
- **Physical midfield** — Jefferson Lerma and Richard Rios provide ball-winning and energy
- **Solid defense** — Davinson Sanchez and Jhon Lucumi form a reliable center-back partnership

## 2026 World Cup Outlook

Colombia enters the 2026 tournament with one of its strongest squads in a generation. With James Rodriguez potentially in his final World Cup at age 34, and Luis Diaz at his peak, the Cafeteros have the talent to make a deep run. A Round of 16 or quarterfinal appearance is realistic, with potential for more if the draw is kind.
