## Skills and Strengths

### Passing Vision
James possesses perhaps the best passing range in Colombian football history. He can deliver every type of pass -- long diagonals, threaded through balls, chipped passes over defenses, and sweeping cross-field balls. His ability to see and execute passes that others cannot conceive is his defining quality.

### Set-Piece Mastery
A dead-ball specialist of the highest order. James's left foot can deliver free kicks, corners, and penalties with extraordinary precision. His free kick technique -- a combination of power, dip, and curve -- has produced goals throughout his career.

### Ball Control and First Touch
James's first touch is world-class, allowing him to receive the ball under pressure and immediately create space. In tight areas, his close control and ability to shift the ball from one foot to the other is mesmerizing.

### Composure in Big Moments
James has an uncanny ability to perform on the biggest stages. From the 2014 World Cup to Copa America finals to Champions League matches, he consistently delivers his best when the stakes are highest.

### Shooting Technique
His left foot generates remarkable power and accuracy from distance. The volley against Uruguay in the 2014 World Cup -- chest control followed by a stunning strike off the underside of the crossbar -- remains one of the greatest goals in World Cup history.

## Defining Moments

### 2014 World Cup Breakout (6 goals, Golden Boot)
The 2014 World Cup was James's career-defining tournament. Six goals in five matches, including the Goal of the Tournament against Uruguay, announced him as a global superstar. He won the Golden Boot and was included in the World Cup All-Star team. Real Madrid signed him immediately after the tournament.

### The Volley vs. Uruguay (2014)
The goal that changed everything. Receiving the ball on his chest 25 yards from goal, James swiveled and struck a stunning volley that crashed in off the underside of the crossbar. It was later voted the goal of the tournament and is regularly featured in lists of the greatest World Cup goals ever scored.

### Real Madrid Years (2014-2020)
James won two Champions League titles and a La Liga crown at Real Madrid. Despite sometimes struggling for regular playing time under Zidane, his quality when given opportunities was undeniable.

## Interesting Facts

### 1. The Name Pronunciation
Despite the English-language spelling of his name, James insists his name is pronounced "HAH-mez" -- the Spanish pronunciation. His father named him after James Bond, a character he admired from the films. The story goes that his father was watching a James Bond marathon on television the night before James was born and decided on the spot that his son would carry the name. James has met several Bond actors and once attended a premiere as a personal guest of the franchise.

### 2. The Nomadic Career
James has played for 11 professional clubs across 8 countries (Colombia, Argentina, Portugal, France, Spain, Germany, England, Qatar, Greece, Brazil, USA) -- making him one of football's most well-traveled elite players. Each move has been driven by different factors: ambition (Real Madrid), opportunity (Everton under Ancelotti), financial (Al-Rayyan), and personal (Minnesota for family reasons). He has stated that he keeps a world map in his home with pins marking every city he has played in, and that his children already speak four languages as a result of their father's global career.
