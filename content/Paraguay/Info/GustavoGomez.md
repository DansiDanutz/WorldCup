## Interesting Facts

### 1. The Only Paraguayan at AC Milan

Gomez is one of very few Paraguayans to have played for **AC Milan**, one of the most storied clubs in world football. He joined the Rossoneri in 2016 from Lanus, becoming the first Paraguayan to wear the famous red and black stripes. While his time at the San Siro was limited to 18 appearances across two seasons -- he struggled to establish himself as a regular starter -- the experience of training alongside legends and playing in Serie A shaped him as a defender. He has said that watching how Italian defenders approached their craft, particularly the emphasis on positioning and anticipation, transformed his game.

### 2. Captain Through the Darkest Times and Back

Gustavo Gomez has been Paraguay's captain through the most difficult period in the nation's recent football history -- the failed qualifying campaigns for the 2014, 2018, and 2022 World Cups. During those years, he was one of the few consistent performers, never losing his commitment to the national team despite the disappointment of missing three consecutive tournaments. When Gustavo Alfaro was appointed in 2024 and began to turn Paraguay's fortunes around, Gomez was the foundation upon which the revival was built. His 88+ caps make him one of Paraguay's most experienced players ever, and leading the team back to the World Cup after 16 years away is his proudest achievement in the white and red shirt.
