## Squad Notes

- **Return after 16 years:** This is Paraguay's first World Cup since 2010
- **Captain fantastic:** Gustavo Gomez (88+ caps) provides leadership and experience from the back
- **Youth and experience:** A good blend of veterans (Gatito Fernandez at 37, Balbuena at 33) and young stars (Diego Gomez at 23, Enciso at 22)
- **Palmeiras connection:** Four players from the Brazilian club (Gomez, Sosa, Mauricio) bring club-level understanding
- **European-based quality:** Alderete (Sunderland), Diego Gomez (Brighton), Enciso (Strasbourg), Sanabria (Cremonese), Caballero (Portsmouth)
- **Notable return:** Isidro Pitta was last called up a year ago but earned his place back in the final squad
