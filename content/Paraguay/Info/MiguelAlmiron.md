## Interesting Facts

### 1. The Record MLS Transfer

When Newcastle United signed Almiron from Atlanta United in January 2019 for a reported **GBP 21 million**, it was both a **Newcastle club record** and the **highest transfer fee ever received by an MLS club** at the time. The transfer was seen as a landmark moment for MLS, proving that the league could produce players worthy of significant Premier League investment. Almiron repaid the faith with six seasons of consistent effort, scoring 30 goals across all competitions and helping Newcastle qualify for the Champions League in the 2022-23 season.

### 2. The MLS Cup Champion Who Conquered England

In 2018, Almiron was the driving force behind Atlanta United's MLS Cup victory in just their second season of existence. His partnership with Josef Martinez was devastating, and he was widely considered the best player in MLS. Rather than stay in the comfort of American football, he chose to test himself in the Premier League -- one of the most demanding leagues in the world. His return to Atlanta United in 2025, after six years in England, was greeted with euphoria by the Atlanta fanbase, who never forgot the player who put their expansion franchise on the map. His homecoming was one of the biggest stories of the 2025 MLS offseason.
