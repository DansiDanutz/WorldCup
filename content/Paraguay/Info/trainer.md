### Key Achievements as Manager

- **Ecuador National Team:** Led Ecuador to qualification for the 2022 FIFA World Cup, one of the most impressive achievements in Ecuadorian football history. Ecuador narrowly missed the Round of 16, drawing with the Netherlands and beating Qatar
- **9-match unbeaten run** with Paraguay in CONMEBOL World Cup qualifying -- a remarkable achievement in the world's most difficult qualifying region
- **Underdog specialist:** Has built a career on getting the maximum out of limited resources, making difficult teams difficult to beat

## Tactical Approach

Alfaro is a **pragmatic, results-oriented coach** whose teams are built on defensive solidity and tactical discipline:

- **Formation:** Primarily **4-4-2** or **4-2-3-1**, with flexibility to shift to a back three when needed
- **Defensive organization:** Teams are extremely well-drilled in their defensive shape, with compact lines and aggressive pressing in the right moments
- **Counter-attacking:** Does not prioritize possession -- instead looks to win the ball and transition quickly, using the pace of players like Enciso, Almiron, and Sosa
- **Set pieces:** Both offensive and defensive set pieces are heavily practiced and a key source of goals
- **Man-marking:** Alfaro sometimes uses man-marking systems in big games to neutralize opposition threats
- **Psychological preparation:** Known for building intense emotional bonds with his players and creating a siege mentality

### Philosophy

Alfaro has described his approach as being built on **"intensity, sacrifice, and collective spirit."** He is not interested in playing beautiful football for its own sake -- he wants results. His teams are characterized by their willingness to suffer without the ball and their explosiveness when they win it back.
