## Interesting Facts

### 1. The Four-League European Education

Alderete has played professional football in **five different countries** -- Paraguay, Argentina, Switzerland, Germany, Spain, and England. This breadth of experience is unusual for a South American defender and has given him a deep tactical education. He has faced every style of forward -- the physical strikers of the Premier League, the technical dribblers of La Liga, the tactical forwards of the Bundesliga, and the tricky wingers of CONMEBOL qualifying. This versatility makes him uniquely suited to a World Cup where opponents from different continents present different challenges.

### 2. The Goal That Helped Secure Qualification

During Paraguay's crucial CONMEBOL qualifying campaign, Alderete scored a vital goal that helped turn the tide in Paraguay's favor. While he is not known as a goal-scoring defender (just 2 international goals), his contributions at both ends of the pitch -- clean sheets, crucial tackles, and the occasional set-piece goal -- were instrumental in Paraguay ending their 16-year World Cup absence. His partnership with Gustavo Gomez at the heart of the defense was the foundation upon which Alfaro built Paraguay's qualifying campaign, and it will be the key to their hopes at the 2026 tournament.
