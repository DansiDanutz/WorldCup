## Interesting Facts

### 1. The Goal That Shocked Manchester

On 18 May 2024, in Brighton's final home game of the Premier League season against Manchester City, Enciso scored a goal that was voted the **Premier League Goal of the Season**. Receiving the ball 30 yards from goal, he unleashed an extraordinary dipping, swerving shot that flew past Stefan Ortega into the top corner. The goal went viral globally, accumulating millions of views within hours. Pep Guardiola was seen applauding on the touchline. For a 20-year-old from Caaguazu, Paraguay, to produce such a moment on one of the world's biggest stages was extraordinary. The goal effectively announced Enciso as a world-class talent.

### 2. From Caaguazu to the Premier League

Enciso grew up in Caaguazu, a small city in central Paraguay with no professional football tradition to speak of. He was spotted by Club Libertad's scouts playing street football and joined their academy as a young teenager. His rise was meteoric -- he was playing in the Paraguayan first division at 15 and had attracted European attention by 17. When Brighton signed him in 2022 for a reported fee of around EUR 10 million, it was one of the largest transfers ever for a Paraguayan teenager. The move from the dusty streets of Caaguazu to the Amex Stadium in the English Premier League is a story that has captured the imagination of every young footballer in Paraguay.
