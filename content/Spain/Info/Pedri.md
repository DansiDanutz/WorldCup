## Career Journey

- **Las Palmas** (2019–2020) — Canarian prodigy, one season in Segunda
- **FC Barcelona** (2020–present) — Koeman promoted him immediately, became starter at 17

### The Xavi Successor
Pedri was compared to Xavi and Iniesta from his debut. His Euro 2020 performance (played every minute, youngest player of the tournament) confirmed him as Spain's creative heartbeat. Despite injury struggles, he remains one of the world's elite midfielders at just 23.

---

## Playing Style

- **Vision**: Extraordinary passing range, always one step ahead
- **Ball retention**: Press-resistant, never loses possession under pressure
- **Work rate**: Covers enormous ground, contributes defensively
- **Composure**: Plays at his own tempo, never rushed
- **Creativity**: Finds spaces that don't seem to exist

---

## Fun Facts

- Born in Tegueste, Tenerife — the Canary Islands
- Played 73 games in the 2020/21 season (club + country + Olympics), the most of any player in Europe
- Won the Golden Boy award in 2021
- His grandmother predicted he would play for Barcelona when he was 3
- Is the youngest player to score for Spain in a major tournament since 1950
