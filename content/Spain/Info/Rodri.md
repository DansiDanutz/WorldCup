## Career Journey

- **Villarreal** (2015–2018) — La Liga breakthrough
- **Atletico Madrid** (2018–2019) — One season, €70M release clause triggered
- **Manchester City** (2019–present) — Treble winner 2023, Ballon d'Or 2024

### The Best in the World
Rodri won the 2024 Ballon d'Or, becoming the first defensive midfielder to win the award in the modern era. He is considered the most important player in both Manchester City and Spain's systems.

---

## Playing Style

- **Ball-winning**: Elite interceptor, reads the game perfectly
- **Passing range**: Metronomic accuracy, rarely gives the ball away
- **Physical presence**: 6'3", dominant in challenges
- **Leadership**: Vocal organizer, sets the tempo
- **Big-game player**: Scores crucial goals (UCL final 2023, Euro 2024 final)

---

## Fun Facts

- His unbeaten run of 74 consecutive games for club and country (2023–2024) is one of the longest in football history
- Won every major trophy available in a single season (2022/23 treble with City)
- His Ballon d'Or win was controversial due to the narrow margin over Vinicius Jr.
- Holds a degree in Business Administration
- Was a ball boy at Atletico Madrid as a child
