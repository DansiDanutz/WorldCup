## Career Journey

- **Athletic Bilbao** (2021–present) — Cantera product, breaking records at San Mames

### Euro 2024 Star
Williams was one of Euro 2024's breakout stars, scoring in the final against England and terrorizing defenses with his pace and directness. His partnership with Lamine Yamal on the opposite flank makes Spain's attack the most exciting in world football.

---

## Playing Style

- **Electric pace**: One of the fastest players in European football
- **Direct running**: Always takes on his man, never passes backwards
- **Dribbling**: Quick feet, devastating in one-on-one situations
- **End product**: Excellent crosser and finisher, not just a speed merchant
- **Pressing**: Works tirelessly without the ball
