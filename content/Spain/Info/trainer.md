## Coaching Career

- **Spain U-19, U-21, U-23** (2013–2022) — Won European Championships at youth levels
- **Spain National Team** (2022–present) — Euro 2024 champions, Nations League winner

### The De la Fuente Revolution
After Luis Enrique's tactical rigidity, De la Fuente brought pragmatism and trust in youth. He led Spain to Euro 2024 glory, proving that La Roja could win with both style and substance.

---

## Tactical Approach

- **4-3-3 formation** with Rodri as the anchor
- **Direct attacking**: Less possession for its own sake, more vertical passing
- **Wing play**: Yamal and Williams stretch defenses wide
- **Youth trust**: Not afraid to start teenagers in major finals
- **Defensive solidity**: Compact shape, Rodri screening the back four
