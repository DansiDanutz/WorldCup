## Career Journey

- **Real Madrid** (2010–2014, 2016–2017) — Won 2 Champions Leagues
- **Juventus** (2014–2016, 2020–2022) — Serie A champion
- **Chelsea** (2017–2019) — Premier League, mixed experience
- **Atletico Madrid** (2019–2020, 2022–2024) — La Liga
- **AC Milan** (2024–2025) — Serie A
- **Como** (2025–present) — Surprising move to newly-promoted Como

---

## Playing Style

- **Movement**: Intelligent runs in behind, constantly stretches defenses
- **Aerial threat**: Good in the air, useful target man
- **Hold-up play**: Brings Yamal, Williams, and Pedri into the game
- **Leadership**: Captain of Spain, emotional leader on and off the pitch
- **Big-game experience**: Champions League, Euros, World Cup veteran

---

## Fun Facts

- Has played for both Real Madrid AND Atletico Madrid (rare crossover)
- His goal celebration involves drawing a "7" with his fingers (his shirt number)
- Has been open about mental health struggles during his Chelsea spell
- Is Spain's all-time top scoring active player
- His move to Como in 2025 shocked the football world
