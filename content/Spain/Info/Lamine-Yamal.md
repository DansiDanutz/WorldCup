## Career Journey

- **FC Barcelona** (2023–present) — Youngest player in Barcelona history (15 years, 9 months), youngest goalscorer in La Liga history

### The Prodigy
Yamal is widely considered the greatest teenage talent since Lionel Messi. At 16, he became Spain's youngest-ever international and goalscorer. By 18, he was a Euro 2024 champion and one of the most valuable players in world football.
