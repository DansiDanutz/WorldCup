### Skills and Strengths

Hirving Lozano is Mexico's most dangerous attacker, a pacey winger who loves to cut inside and shoot.

**Key strengths:**
- **Pace**: Among the fastest players in CONCACAF
- **Cutting inside**: Lethal right-footed shots from the left wing
- **Dribbling**: Takes on defenders with confidence
- **Big-game goals**: Scored winner against Germany in 2018
- **Experience**: Played at PSV, Napoli, and two World Cups
- **Counter-attacking**: Devastating on the break
