## Career Journey

### Club Career
- **Club America** (2016–2019) — Broke through as a versatile defender/midfielder, won Liga MX
- **Ajax Amsterdam** (2019–2023) — Became club captain, won 3 Eredivisie titles, Champions League semi-finalist
- **West Ham United** (2023–2025) — Premier League move, Europa Conference League winner
- **Fenerbahce** (2025–present) — Turkish Super Lig

### Leadership
Alvarez became Ajax's first non-European captain in decades, a testament to his leadership qualities and tactical intelligence. He's widely regarded as Mexico's most important player — the team's entire structure depends on his presence in midfield.

## Playing Style

- **Ball-winning**: Elite tackler and interceptor, reads the game brilliantly
- **Versatility**: Can play CB, CDM, or even RB
- **Passing range**: Comfortable on the ball, good long-passing ability
- **Leadership**: Vocal organizer, sets the tempo for the entire team
- **Physicality**: Strong in challenges, rarely loses aerial duels
