## Career Journey

### Club Career
- **Cruz Azul** (2017–2022) — Developed through youth academy, became starter at 19
- **Feyenoord** (2022–2025) — Explosion into European football, 58 goals in 92 appearances, won Eredivisie (2023)
- **AC Milan** (2025–present) — Big-money move to Serie A, Mexico's most expensive export

### Breakthrough
Gimenez was the **top Mexican scorer in European history** within his first two seasons at Feyenoord, surpassing legends like Javier Hernandez's early records. His clinical finishing and intelligent movement made him one of Europe's most sought-after young strikers.

## Playing Style

- **Fox in the box**: Instinctive goal scorer, always in the right place
- **First-time finishing**: Exceptional one-touch finishing ability
- **Movement**: Ghost-like movement in the penalty area
- **Aerial ability**: Good in the air despite average height
- **Pressing**: High work rate, presses from the front

## Fun Facts

- Son of former Mexico striker Christian "Chaco" Gimenez
- Scored on his Champions League debut for Feyenoord
- Was the Eredivisie's top-scoring Mexican of all time
- Known for his humble personality despite his rapid rise to fame
- His nickname "Bebelo" comes from his father's nickname for him as a baby
