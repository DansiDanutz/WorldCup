## Career Journey

### Club Career
- **Seattle Sounders** (2021–2024) — MLS homegrown signing at 15, youngest player in Sounders history
- **Atletico Madrid** (2024–present) — High-profile move to La Liga, one of Mexico's brightest young talents

### Rise to Prominence
Vargas burst onto the scene as a teenager in MLS, becoming the youngest player to appear in an MLS Cup final. His vision, passing range, and maturity beyond his years attracted attention from top European clubs. Atletico Madrid won the bidding war, and Diego Simeone has called him "the future of Mexican football."

## Playing Style

- **Vision**: Exceptional passing range, sees passes others don't
- **Composure**: Plays with the calm of a veteran despite being just 20
- **Press resistance**: Comfortable receiving the ball under pressure
- **Dual-footed**: Equally comfortable with left and right foot
- **Progressive passing**: Always looking to play forward
