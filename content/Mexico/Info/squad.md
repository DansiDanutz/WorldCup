## Squad Analysis

### Strengths
- **Home advantage**: Hosting provides passionate crowds and familiar conditions
- **Edson Alvarez**: Elite defensive midfielder at West Ham
- **Lozano experience**: World Cup veteran who brings big-game pedigree
- **Gimenez potential**: Rising star at AC Milan
- **Aguirre's know-how**: Third-time manager who understands Mexican football

### Weaknesses
- **Goalkeeper uncertainty**: Post-Ochoa era; no established elite keeper
- **Striker inconsistency**: Gimenez is young; Martin is aging
- **Defensive frailty**: Can be exposed by pacey attackers
- **Pressure of hosting**: Enormous expectations from passionate fans
- **Lack of elite wingers**: Beyond Lozano, creativity is limited
