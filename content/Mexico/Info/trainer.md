### Tactical Approach

Aguirre is a pragmatic manager who prioritizes defensive organization:
- **4-4-2 foundation**: Solid, compact defensive shape
- **Counter-attacking**: Quick transitions from defense to attack
- **Set-piece focus**: Well-drilled routines
- **Experience**: Knows Mexican football culture intimately
- **Tournament mentality**: Understands how to manage knockout football
