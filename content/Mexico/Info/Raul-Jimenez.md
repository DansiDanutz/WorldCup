## Career Journey

### Club Career
- **Club America** (2011–2014) — Broke through as a young striker, won Liga MX
- **Atletico Madrid** (2014–2015) — Limited minutes behind Mandzukic and Griezmann
- **Benfica** (2015–2018) — Found his scoring boots in Portugal, won Primeira Liga
- **Wolverhampton** (2018–2023) — Career peak: 57 goals in 166 appearances, cult hero status
- **Fulham** (2023–present) — Premier League revival, reliable goal scorer

### The Skull Fracture (2020)
In November 2020, Jimenez suffered a **fractured skull** in a collision with Arsenal's David Luiz. He underwent emergency surgery and returned to football just 8 months later with a protective headband — one of the most remarkable comebacks in Premier League history.

## Playing Style

- **Aerial threat**: Dominant in the air, excellent header of the ball
- **Hold-up play**: Strong back-to-goal play, brings others into attack
- **Penalty specialist**: Calm from the spot, near-perfect conversion rate
- **Link-up play**: Surprisingly good technique for a target man
