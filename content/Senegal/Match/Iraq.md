# Senegal vs Iraq — Group I, Matchday 2

**Date:** June 17, 2026 | **Tournament:** FIFA World Cup 2026

---

## Head-to-Head History

Senegal and Iraq have never met in an official FIFA match.

---

## The Storyline: The Battle for Survival

Both Senegal and Iraq view this match as crucial for their knockout-round hopes. With France looming as the group favorite, this head-to-head could determine who advances.

Senegal's World Cup pedigree (2002 quarter-final, 2022 Round of 16) gives them the edge. Their squad features players from Chelsea, Bayern Munich, and other elite clubs. Iraq counters with collective spirit forged through adversity — the same spirit that won them the 2007 Asian Cup against all odds.

The Lions of Teranga against the Lions of Mesopotamia. Two proud footballing nations fighting for survival. The passion will be extraordinary.

## Key Player Matchups

### Sadio Mane vs Iraq's Right Side
Mane's movement against Iraq's organized defense. The Senegalese star's experience at the highest level could be the difference.

### Nicolas Jackson vs Iraq's Center-Backs
Jackson's pace and pressing against Iraq's deep block. The Chelsea striker's energy will test Iraq's defensive discipline.

### Kalidou Koulibaly vs Aymen Hussein
Koulibaly's defensive class against Hussein's finishing. The Senegalese captain should dominate this matchup.

### Lamine Camara vs Zidane Iqbal
Camara's energy against Iqbal's technical quality. A midfield battle between two of the game's promising young talents.

## Tactical Preview

**Senegal (4-3-3):** Organized and athletic. Counter-attacks through Mane and Jackson. Set pieces are a weapon with Koulibaly's aerial presence. Senegal's quality should tell over 90 minutes.

**Iraq (5-4-1):** Deep defensive block, maximum discipline. Counter through Iqbal's creativity and Hussein's finishing. Physical defending.
