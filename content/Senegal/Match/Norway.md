## The Storyline: Stars Collide

Erling Haaland vs Kalidou Koulibaly. Martin Odegaard vs Sadio Mane. Two genuine superstars on each side, surrounded by quality supporting casts. This is the kind of match that makes World Cup group stages unmissable.

For both teams, this match likely determines knockout advancement. The group-stage finale carries a unique tension — both teams know exactly what they need, and neither can afford a mistake.
