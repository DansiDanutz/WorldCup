- Thiaw's preferred 4-3-3 gives maximum freedom to the wide forwards -- Mane and Ismaila Sarr are the primary threats
- Lamine Camara (market value 40 million euros) is the midfield jewel -- technically gifted and tactically mature at just 22
- Koulibaly's leadership at the back is irreplaceable; Mamadou Sarr (19, Chelsea) represents the future
- Nicolas Jackson and Iliman Ndiaye offer different attacking options from the bench
- Bara Sapoko Ndiaye's inclusion after impressing against the USA is a bold selection that rewards youth
- The squad blends AFCON-winning experience with exciting young talent
