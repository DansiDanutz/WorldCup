## Interesting Facts and Stories

### 1. From Manchester United Academy to Sweden Star

Elanga came through the famed Manchester United academy, making his first-team debut under Ralf Rangnick in 2022. He scored in the Champions League against Atletico Madrid as a 19-year-old, announcing himself on the European stage. After falling down the pecking order at United, he moved to Nottingham Forest in 2023 and became one of their most important players, contributing heavily with goals and assists. His performances earned him a move to Newcastle United in 2025. Throughout his club career changes, he has remained a constant in the Sweden national team setup.

### 2. The Cameroonian Connection

Elanga's father, Joseph Elanga, was a professional footballer who played for Cameroon and featured in the 1998 World Cup. Despite being eligible to play for Cameroon, Anthony chose to represent Sweden, the country of his birth. He has spoken about the pride he feels in representing Sweden while also honoring his Cameroonian heritage. His father's experience as a World Cup player has given Anthony unique perspective and advice heading into the 2026 tournament -- father and son now share the distinction of having played on football's biggest stage.
