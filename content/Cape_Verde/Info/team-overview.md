### The Blue Sharks

Cape Verde's qualification for the 2026 World Cup is a triumph for African football's smaller nations. An archipelago of just 600,000 people off the coast of West Africa, Cape Verde has punched far above its weight.

### The Diaspora Advantage

## Playing Style

Cape Verde plays a **disciplined 4-4-2** emphasizing:
- **Defensive solidarity**: Well-organized and hard to break down
- **Pace on the wings**: Quick, skillful wide players
- **Set-piece threat**: Physical team that scores from dead balls
- **Counter-attacking**: Hits teams on the break
- **Tactical intelligence**: European-based players understand systems
