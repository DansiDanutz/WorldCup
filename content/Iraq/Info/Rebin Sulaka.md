## Funny and Interesting Facts

### 1. The Swedish-Iraqi Journeyman

Sulaka was born and raised in Sweden but chose to represent Iraq, the country of his parents' origin. His career has taken him across four continents -- from the Swedish lower leagues to Qatar, China, and Thailand. He has joked that he has "played in more countries than most people have visited." His nomadic career has given him an understanding of different football cultures that has made him a natural leader in Iraq's diverse, globally scattered squad.

### 2. The Lucky Headband

Sulaka is superstitious about wearing a specific black headband during matches. He started wearing it during a qualifier where Iraq kept a clean sheet, and has refused to play without it since. Teammates have tried to hide it as a prank before matches, causing mild panic in the locker room. When a journalist asked him about it, Sulaka deadpanned: "It is not the headband that makes me play well. It is the fear of losing the headband that makes me concentrate harder."
