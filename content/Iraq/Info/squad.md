# Iraq - 2026 World Cup Squad

## Squad Overview

Head coach **Graham Arnold** named an experienced 26-man squad that relies heavily on the core group of players who secured qualification through the playoff against Bolivia. The squad features the first Iraqi to play in the English Premier League (Ali Al-Hamadi) and a former Manchester United academy product (Zidane Iqbal).
