## Funny and Interesting Facts

### 1. Detained for 7 Hours at US Airport

When Iraq arrived in the United States for the 2026 World Cup, Aymen Hussein was held and questioned for nearly **7 hours** at Chicago's O'Hare International Airport. The incident made headlines worldwide, with The Guardian, BBC, and Reuters all covering the story. Hussein reportedly remained calm and cooperative throughout, later telling teammates: "I have waited 40 years for this World Cup -- what is a few more hours?" The Iraqi Football Federation formally protested the treatment.

### 2. Qualifying Hero

Hussein scored the most dramatic goal in Iraqi football history since Younis Mahmoud's 2007 Asian Cup winner. In the playoff final against Bolivia, Hussein netted the decisive goal that sealed Iraq's return to the World Cup for the first time in 40 years. The goal sparked wild celebrations across Iraq, with fans taking to the streets in Baghdad, Basra, and Erbil. Hussein later revealed he had promised his grandmother -- who passed away before qualification -- that he would take Iraq to the World Cup. He dedicated the goal to her memory.
