**Date:** June 22, 2026 | **Tournament:** FIFA World Cup 2026

## Head-to-Head History

Iraq and Norway have never met in an official FIFA match.

---

## The Storyline: Pride and Farewell

Iraq's World Cup campaign concludes against a Norway side featuring two of football's biggest stars. For Iraq, this is about pride — showing the world they belong, regardless of the group standings.

The challenge is immense. Erling Haaland's movement and Martin Odegaard's creativity represent a level of individual quality that Iraq's defense has rarely faced. But Iraq has built their football identity on collective effort overcoming individual limitations.

For Norway, this match is about securing advancement and building momentum. A professional, efficient performance is expected — but Iraq's fighting spirit could make this anything but routine.

**Norway (4-2-3-1):** Direct to Haaland, create through Odegaard. Comfortable attacking against a deep block. Set pieces are a weapon.
