# Iraq vs Senegal — Group I, Matchday 2

**Date:** June 17, 2026 | **Tournament:** FIFA World Cup 2026

---

## Head-to-Head History

Iraq and Senegal have never met in an official FIFA match.

---

## The Storyline: The Swing Match for Survival

Both Iraq and Senegal will view this match as their best opportunity for points in Group I. With France likely too strong, this head-to-head could determine who finishes third — and potentially advances as one of the best third-place teams.

Senegal's tournament pedigree (2002 quarter-finalists, 2022 Round of 16) gives them the edge on paper. But Iraq's collective spirit, forged through adversity, cannot be measured by statistics.

The 2007 Asian Cup victory proved Iraq can overcome superior opposition through unity and determination. Against Senegal, they need to summon that spirit one more time.

---

## Key Player Matchups

### Zidane Iqbal vs Lamine Camara
Iqbal's technical quality against Camara's emerging talent. The midfield battle sets the tone.

### Aymen Hussein vs Kalidou Koulibaly
Hussein's finishing against Koulibaly's defensive leadership. The veteran knows how to shut down strikers.

### Sadio Mane vs Iraq's Right Side
Mane's experience against Iraq's organized defense. Even in his 30s, Mane's movement is world-class.

### Ali Al-Hamadi vs Pape Thiaw's Defense
Al-Hamadi's direct running against Senegal's backline. Iraq must create through wide areas.

---

## Tactical Preview

**Iraq (5-4-1):** Deep block, maximum discipline. Counter through Iqbal's creativity and Hussein's finishing. Physical defending.

**Senegal (4-3-3):** Organized defense, rapid counters through Mane and Jackson. Senegal's quality across the pitch should tell over 90 minutes.

---

## X-Factor

**Iraq's 2007 spirit.** The memory of the Asian Cup triumph lives in every Iraqi player. If they can channel that unity and belief, they can compete with anyone.
