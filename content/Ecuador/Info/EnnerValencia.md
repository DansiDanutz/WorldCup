## Interesting Facts

### Fact 1: The 2022 World Cup Opening Match Hero
At the 2022 World Cup in Qatar, Enner Valencia wrote his name into football history by scoring both goals in Ecuador's 2-0 victory over the host nation in the tournament's opening match. This made Ecuador the first team ever to beat the host nation in the opening match of a World Cup. Valencia scored a header and a penalty, dominating the match and silencing the home crowd. The performance was particularly sweet because many had written off the 33-year-old before the tournament. His total of 3 goals in the 2022 group stage matched his tally from 2014, making him one of only a few players to score 3+ goals in two different World Cups.

### Fact 2: The Great Escape Incident
During his time at West Ham United in 2016, Valencia was involved in one of football's most bizarre episodes. After an international match, Ecuadorian authorities attempted to detain him over an alleged unpaid child support dispute. Valencia was dramatically prevented from leaving the team bus, with police surrounding it at the airport. The situation was eventually resolved, but the incident made global headlines and became part of Valencia's folklore. He later joked about it in interviews, saying: "I just wanted to play football — I didn't expect to need lawyers at the airport." The incident did not affect his international career, and he continued to be selected for Ecuador, eventually becoming captain.
