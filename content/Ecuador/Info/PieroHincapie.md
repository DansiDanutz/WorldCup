## Interesting Facts

### Fact 1: The GPS-Breaking Defender
During his time at Bayer Leverkusen, Hincapie reportedly broke GPS tracking devices worn during training — not by damaging them intentionally, but by producing explosive acceleration and sprint data that exceeded the devices' recording capacity. Sky Sports covered the story, calling him a "physical beast" and noting that his athletic metrics were off the charts. The story went viral among football fans, with many comparing his physical profile to Virgil van Dijk at the same age. When Arsenal signed him on loan in September 2025, the GPS story was frequently cited as evidence of his extraordinary physical capabilities.

### Fact 2: The Leverkusen Invincible
Hincapie was part of the historic Bayer Leverkusen team that went unbeaten in the 2023/24 Bundesliga season — one of the greatest achievements in German football history. Under Xabi Alonso, Leverkusen won the league without losing a single match, a feat never before accomplished in the Bundesliga. Hincapie was a key part of that defense, playing as both centre-back and left-back throughout the campaign. He also won the DFB-Pokal that season, completing a domestic double. His performances earned him the move to Arsenal on loan, with a 45 million pound option to buy that many consider a bargain for a defender of his quality.
