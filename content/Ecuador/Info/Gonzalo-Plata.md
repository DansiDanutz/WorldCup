## Playing Style

Plata is an electrifying winger with explosive pace and trickery. His direct running style and ability to beat defenders one-on-one make him a key outlet for Ecuador on the counter-attack. He combines South American flair with increasing tactical discipline.

## Key Skills
- **Dribbling:** Quick feet and close control to beat multiple defenders
- **Pace:** Explosive acceleration in short distances
- **Crossing:** Accurate delivery from both flanks
- **Versatility:** Comfortable on either wing or as a second striker
- **Big-Game Mentality:** Has delivered in crucial qualifiers and tournaments
