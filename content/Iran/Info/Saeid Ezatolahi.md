## Funny and Interesting Facts

### 1. Youngest Goalscorer in Iran's History

Ezatolahi became the **youngest goalscorer in the history of the Iran national team** at just **19 years and 42 days old**. He scored in a 3-1 victory, a record that still stands. What makes this even more remarkable is that he is primarily a defensive midfielder -- not known for scoring. His historic goal came from a rare foray forward, and he has only scored one more international goal since, making that debut strike even more special.

### 2. The Atletico Madrid Connection

In 2017, Ezatolahi went on loan to Atletico Madrid's B team, becoming one of the few Iranian players to be associated with one of Spain's biggest clubs. Although he never made a first-team appearance for Atletico, he trained alongside players like Antoine Griezmann and Koke, an experience he has described as "transformative" for his understanding of European tactical systems. Diego Simeone reportedly praised his attitude and physicality in training sessions.
