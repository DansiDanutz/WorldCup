## Funny and Interesting Facts

### 1. The Bicycle Kick That Shocked Europe

In September 2020, Taremi scored an incredible bicycle kick for FC Porto against Chelsea in the UEFA Champions League. The goal went viral worldwide and was nominated for the FIFA Puskas Award. What made it even more remarkable was that Taremi had only been at Porto for a few weeks after arriving from Iranian football via a brief spell in Qatar. He went from relative obscurity in European football to scoring one of the goals of the season against a Champions League giant.

### 2. Late Bloomer Who Never Gave Up

Unlike many elite strikers who shine from their teens, Taremi did not make his breakthrough until he was 22. He was playing in Iran's lower divisions and worked at a gas station in Bushehr to support himself while pursuing football. When he finally got his chance at Persepolis at age 22, he seized it, becoming the league's top scorer and eventually working his way to the Champions League -- proving it is never too late to follow your dreams.
