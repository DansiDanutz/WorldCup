# Iran - 2026 World Cup Squad

## Squad Overview

Head coach **Amir Ghalenoei** finalized Iran's 26-man squad during the team's training camp in Antalya, Turkiye, ahead of the June 2 FIFA deadline. The biggest headline was the **omission of veteran striker Sardar Azmoun** from the final selection.
