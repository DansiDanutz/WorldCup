## Funny and Interesting Facts

### 1. The Full-Back Who Saved the Season

During an injury crisis at Brentford in the 2022-23 Premier League season, Ghoddos was asked to play at full-back -- a position he had never played professionally. Not only did he acquit himself well, but he put in a Player of the Match performance against a top-six side, making tackles, interceptions, and even creating a goal from his unfamiliar position. Thomas Frank later said: "Only Saman could go from attacking midfielder to full-back and look like he had played there his whole career."

### 2. Born in Sweden, Chose Iran

Ghoddos was born and raised in Malmo, Sweden, and represented Sweden at youth level before switching his international allegiance to Iran in 2017. The decision was deeply personal -- his parents are Iranian, and he grew up in a household that celebrated both Swedish and Iranian culture. He has said that playing for Iran "felt like coming home," and his fluency in both Swedish and Persian has made him a beloved figure among Iranian fans in the diaspora.
