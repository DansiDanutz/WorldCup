## Funny and Interesting Facts

### 1. The Boxing Day Bicycle Kick

On December 26, 2019, Jahanbakhsh scored a stunning bicycle kick for Brighton against Chelsea in the Premier League. It was only his second Premier League goal after a long drought following his big-money move from AZ Alkmaar. The goal went viral, and Jahanbakhsh later admitted he had practiced bicycle kicks in training but never expected to pull one off in a competitive match -- especially against Chelsea at Stamford Bridge.

### 2. Eredivisie Top Scorer as a Winger

In the 2017-18 season, Jahanbakhsh won the Eredivisie Golden Boot with 21 goals -- a remarkable achievement for a player primarily deployed as a winger. This made him the first Asian player to finish as the Eredivisie's top scorer, and it earned him a transfer to Brighton for a reported club-record fee. He beat established strikers to the crown, scoring from wide positions with a combination of cutting inside, long-range shooting, and aerial ability.
