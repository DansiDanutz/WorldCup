## Squad Analysis

### Strengths
- **Messi factor**: The greatest player of all time provides moments of magic no system can contain
- **Championship DNA**: Core group has won 3 consecutive major tournaments -- they know how to win
- **Midfield engine**: Mac Allister, Enzo Fernandez, De Paul form one of the best midfields in the tournament
- **Goalkeeping**: Emiliano Martinez thrives in high-pressure moments
- **Attacking depth**: Lautaro Martinez, Julian Alvarez, Thiago Almada offer different profiles

### Weaknesses
- **Age in key positions**: Messi (38), Otamendi (38), Paredes (31), Tagliafico (33)
- **Right-back**: Montiel and Molina are solid but not world-class
- **Pace at the back**: Otamendi can be exposed by fast attackers
- **Messi dependency**: When Messi has an off day, the team's attacking output drops significantly

### Key Tactical Setup
Scaloni is expected to use a **4-3-3 or 4-4-2**:
- Emiliano Martinez in goal
- Romero and Lisandro Martinez as the CB pairing
- Mac Allister, Enzo Fernandez, De Paul as the midfield trio
- Messi operating as a free-roaming playmaker/forward
- Julian Alvarez or Lautaro Martinez as the main striker
