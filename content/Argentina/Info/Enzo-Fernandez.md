### Skills and Strengths

Enzo Fernandez burst onto the global stage at the 2022 World Cup, where he was named the **Best Young Player** of the tournament. Since then, he has established himself as one of the most complete midfielders in the Premier League.

**Key strengths:**
- **Tackling**: Tenacious ball-winner; his timing in challenges is exceptional
- **Passing range**: Can play short, intricate passes or launch 50-yard diagonals
- **Energy**: Covers every blade of grass; box-to-box engine
- **Technical quality**: Comfortable receiving under pressure; rarely misplaces a pass
- **Aerial ability**: Surprisingly good in the air for his height
- **Leadership**: Despite his young age, he organizes and drives the midfield

### Funny and Interesting Facts

**1. The Record-Breaking Transfer**
Chelsea paid a British-record fee (reported at 107 million pounds) to sign Enzo from Benfica in January 2023. The negotiations went down to the final seconds of transfer deadline day, with paperwork submitted just minutes before the window closed. Enzo later revealed he was watching a movie with his family when his agent called to say: "Pack your bags, you're going to London." He arrived at Chelsea's training ground the next morning without having had time to pack properly.

**2. Named After Enzo Francescoli**
Enzo was named after the legendary Uruguayan footballer Enzo Francescoli, who was his father Raul's idol. Raul Fernandez, himself a former footballer, was such a huge Francescoli fan that he named his son after the Uruguayan star. Ironically, young Enzo grew up to become a star for Argentina -- Uruguay's biggest rival. When asked about this, Enzo laughed: "My dad is a man of his word. He loved Francescoli, but he loves Argentina more."
