### Skills and Strengths

Emiliano Martinez is the ultimate big-game goalkeeper. His shot-stopping, penalty-saving heroics, and psychological warfare have made him one of the most feared goalkeepers in world football.

**Key strengths:**
- **Penalty saving**: Arguably the best penalty saver in football history; studies takers meticulously
- **Psychological edge**: Uses mind games, gestures, and words to unsettle opponents
- **Shot-stopping**: World-class reflexes; makes point-blank saves look routine
- **Command of area**: Dominates his box; punches and catches crosses with authority
- **Distribution**: Excellent with his feet; can launch counters with long throws
- **Big-game temperament**: Actually performs BETTER in high-pressure moments

### Funny and Interesting Facts

**1. The Golden Glove Trophy Gesture**
After winning the Golden Glove at the 2022 World Cup, Martinez caused global controversy by placing the trophy near his groin area during the awards ceremony. The gesture was a reference to a promise he made to his teammates: "If we win, I'll do something nobody will forget." FIFA reportedly fined him, but Martinez was unrepentant: "I did it for the lads. We had a laugh about it in the dressing room. That's what matters."

**2. The "Look at Me" Penalty Routine**
Martinez has developed a terrifying pre-penalty routine where he stares directly at the taker, taps his gloves together slowly, and whispers something inaudible. Multiple players have admitted they changed their intended penalty placement because of his intimidation. He once revealed: "I don't say anything bad. Sometimes I just say 'look at me, look at me.' It's enough to make them nervous."
