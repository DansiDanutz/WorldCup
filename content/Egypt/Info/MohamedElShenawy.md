## Interesting Facts

### Fact 1: Al Ahly's Iron Man
Mohamed El Shenawy has been Al Ahly's first-choice goalkeeper for nearly a decade, during which time the club has dominated Egyptian and African football. Al Ahly is the most successful club in African football history, and El Shenawy has been their last line of defense through multiple CAF Champions League triumphs. He has kept well over 100 clean sheets for the club across all competitions. Despite lucrative offers from European and Gulf clubs over the years, he has remained loyal to Al Ahly, becoming a club icon alongside other Egyptian legends.

### Fact 2: The Beard That Became a Symbol
El Shenawy's distinctive long beard has become his trademark and a symbol recognized across Egypt. During the 2018 World Cup, his beard and performances drew comparisons to Liverpool goalkeeper Alisson (who also sported facial hair). Egyptian fans created memes and social media accounts dedicated to "El Shenawy's beard," treating it almost as a separate entity. He has reportedly been offered significant money to appear in shaving product advertisements but has declined, keeping his beard as a personal and religious choice. It has become so iconic that young Egyptian goalkeepers grow beards in imitation.
