- **Nickname:** "Musa"

- **Date of Birth:** June 10, 1997
- **Place of Birth:** Amman, Jordan

## Playing Style

Al-Taamari is Jordan's star player — a dazzling winger with electrifying pace and dribbling ability. The first Jordanian to play in Ligue 1, he is the heartbeat of the national team and one of Asia's most exciting players.

## Key Skills
- **Dribbling:** Mesmeric close control and trickery
- **Pace:** Lightning acceleration
- **Creativity:** Creates chances out of nothing
- **Big-Game Temperament:** Delivers in crucial moments
- **Versatility:** Can play on either wing or as a #10
