# Hussein Ammouta — Head Coach

## Profile
- **Full Name:** Hussein Ammouta
- **Date of Birth:** October 2, 1969
- **Place of Birth:** Errachidia, Morocco
- **Nationality:** Moroccan
- **Appointed:** 2023

## Coaching Career
Ammouta has managed across the Arab world:
- Al-Hilal (Saudi Arabia)
- Wydad AC (Morocco) — CAF Champions League winner
- Qatar SC (Qatar)
- Jordan National Team (2023-present)

## Tactical Philosophy
- **Defensive Solidity:** Organized 4-2-3-1 or 5-3-2
- **Counter-Attacking:** Rapid transitions using Al-Taamari's pace
- **Set Pieces:** Heavily drilled offensive and defensive routines
- **Team Spirit:** Builds incredible unity and belief

## Achievements
- Led Jordan to 2024 Asian Cup final (best-ever result)
- CAF Champions League winner with Wydad AC
- Qualified Jordan for their first-ever FIFA World Cup
