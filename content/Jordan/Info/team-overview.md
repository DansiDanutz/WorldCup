# Jordan National Football Team — Al-Nashama

## Overview

The Jordan national football team, known as **Al-Nashama** (The Brave), has emerged as one of Asia's rising football nations. Jordan reached the Asian Cup final in 2024, their best-ever result, and qualified for their first-ever FIFA World Cup in 2026.

## Group J: Argentina, Algeria, Austria, Jordan

Jordan's World Cup debut comes with a draw worthy of the occasion: world champions Argentina, North African rivals Algeria, and a well-drilled Austria stand between Al-Nashama and the knockout rounds.

## Playing Style

Jordan plays a disciplined, defensively organized system that relies on counter-attacking and set pieces. Under manager Hussein Ammouta, they became difficult to beat and clinical on the break.

### Key Tactical Elements
- **Defensive Organization:** Low block with disciplined positioning
- **Counter-Attacking:** Rapid transitions using pace of Musa Al-Taamari
- **Set Pieces:** Major offensive weapon
- **Team Spirit:** Incredible togetherness and fighting spirit
