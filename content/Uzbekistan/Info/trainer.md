# Srečko Katanec — Head Coach

## Profile
- **Full Name:** Srečko Katanec
- **Date of Birth:** July 3, 1963
- **Place of Birth:** Ljubljana, Slovenia
- **Nationality:** Slovenian
- **Appointed:** 2021

## Playing Career
Katanec was a versatile midfielder who played for Partizan Belgrade, Stuttgart, and Sampdoria, winning the European Cup Winners' Cup. He earned 31 caps for Yugoslavia.

## Coaching Career
| Period | Role | Team |
|--------|------|------|
| 1998-2002 | Head Coach | Slovenia National Team (Euro 2000, World Cup 2002) |
| 2006-2009 | Head Coach | Macedonia National Team |
| 2009-2011 | Head Coach | UAE National Team |
| 2012-2017 | Head Coach | Iraq National Team |
| 2021-Present | Head Coach | Uzbekistan National Team |

## Tactical Philosophy
- **Defensive Organization:** Structured 4-2-3-1 or 4-3-3
- **Discipline:** Tactical discipline across all positions
- **Counter-Attacking:** Uses pace of wide players on the break
- **Physicality:** Leverages the natural athleticism of the squad

## Achievements
- Led Slovenia to Euro 2000 and World Cup 2002 (first-ever tournaments)
- Led Uzbekistan to their first-ever World Cup qualification
