### Skills and Strengths

Abdukodir Khusanov is the brightest talent in Uzbek football history — a composed, athletic centre-back who became the first Uzbek to play for Manchester City.

**Key strengths:**
- **Composure**: Plays with the calm of a veteran twice his age
- **Passing**: Excellent ball-playing ability from the back
- **Physicality**: Strong in challenges and aerial duels
- **Reading the game**: Anticipates danger and intercepts passes
- **Potential**: Considered one of the best young defenders in world football
