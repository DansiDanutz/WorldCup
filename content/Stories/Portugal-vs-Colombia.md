### 4 · THE 84TH MINUTE (2:35–3:50)
**[MUSIC: both themes lift — then total silence as James controls it.]**

He kills it with one touch — *that first touch, his superpower* — and curls a shot toward the top corner. Diogo Costa flies, gets fingertips to it, pushes it onto the bar. **[SUPPORTERS: a gasp, then a roar.]** James looks to the heavens and smiles. *Even the gods appreciate beautiful football.*

### 6 · THE MYSTERY, UNDERLINED (4:25–5:00)
**[MUSIC: the two number-ten themes hold one unresolved chord.]**
