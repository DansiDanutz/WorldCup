### 1 · COLD OPEN — The Argument Made Flesh (0:00–0:55)
**[MUSIC: two heavyweight themes enter from opposite channels and circle.]**
**[ANIMATION: split screen — Mbappé's blur of speed; Haaland's metronomic finishing — slamming together at the centre circle.]**

Levi's Stadium hosts the most anticipated group match of the entire tournament. France versus Norway is not just a match — it is Kylian Mbappé versus Erling Haaland, the two most devastating forwards of their generation, finally meeting on the World Cup stage. *(beat)* The debate has raged for years: who is better — the electric Frenchman, or the Norwegian goal machine? Tonight, the argument stops being words.

**[SUPPORTERS — Norwegian end, war-horns: "Hei-a Nor-ge!"]**

### 2 · THE ELECTRIC PRINCE (0:55–1:45)
**[MUSIC: French electricity — fast, bright, dangerous.]**
**[ANIMATION: Mbappé exploding forward, 38 km/h, defenders peeling away; children's bedroom walls covered in his image.]**

Mbappé stands at the centre circle, Real Madrid white gleaming. At 27, in his prime — 38 km/h pace, clinical finishing, the most feared forward alive. He grew up covering his walls with Ronaldo posters; now children everywhere cover theirs with him. Told a teenage Messi was watching him play, he simply shrugged: *"It's not bad, Messi is watching me."* Self-belief that borders on the supernatural.

### 3 · THE GOAL MACHINE (1:45–2:35)
**[MUSIC: Norwegian Viking percussion — heavy, cold, relentless.]**
**[ANIMATION: Haaland, 6'4", casually powerful; Ødegaard threading the strings behind him.]**

Haaland stretches on the touchline with the casual power of a man with 120-plus goals in 130-plus City games — the most prolific scorer in Premier League history per game. Finishing unmatched, movement intelligent, mentality ice-cold. Behind him, Martin Ødegaard, Norway's captain, pulls the strings with an Arsenal elegance that unlocks any defence. *Two unstoppable forces. One pitch.*

**[SUPPORTERS — full Norwegian roar: "Hei-a Nor-ge! Hei-a Nor-ge!"]**

### 4 · THE 72ND MINUTE (2:35–3:50)
**[MUSIC: both themes lock — then total silence on the strike.]**
**[ANIMATION: 1–1; Mbappé's burst, the cut inside, the save; the rebound to Ødegaard; the long ball; Haaland and Saliba as two freight trains on parallel tracks; the first-time strike screaming toward the top corner.]**

72nd minute. 1–1. Mbappé takes it on the left and accelerates — that burst. Haaland, watching from the circle, starts his own run. Two freight trains on parallel tracks. Mbappé beats his man, cuts inside, shoots — the keeper saves. The rebound falls to Ødegaard, sprinted all the way back, who plays it long. Haaland chases; Saliba matches him stride for stride; the ball arrives, and Haaland strikes first time. **The ball screams toward the top corner.** **[SUPPORTERS: the crowd rises as one.]**

### 5 · NOTHING ELSE MATTERS (3:50–4:25)
**[MUSIC: the two themes burn at equal volume.]**
**[ANIMATION: the whole stadium on its feet; the group table irrelevant, forgotten.]**

Two titans, one stage. When the world stops to watch Mbappé and Haaland test each other's limits, nothing else matters — not the standings, not the permutations, not the mathematics. *(beat)* This is why the World Cup exists: for the nights the two greatest players on earth refuse to let the other be the best.

### 6 · THE MYSTERY, UNDERLINED (4:25–5:00)
**[MUSIC: electricity and Viking percussion hold one unresolved chord.]**
**[ANIMATION: the ball frozen in flight; Mbappé and Haaland, half a pitch apart, both watching it.]**

The debate, gloriously, lives on.

**[SUPPORTERS — fading, overlapping: "Allez les Bleus!" … "Hei-a Nor-ge!"]**
