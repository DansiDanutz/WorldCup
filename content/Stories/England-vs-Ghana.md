**[MUSIC: Ghanaian highlife guitar over rising drums.]**

Iñaki Williams, who chose Ghana over Spain, brings explosive pace and a refusal of limits. And Antoine Semenyo carries Premier League pedigree *into a match against the very country that invented the league he dominates.*
