# Gabriel Calderón — Head Coach

## Profile
- **Full Name:** Gabriel Humberto Calderón
- **Date of Birth:** February 7, 1960
- **Place of Birth:** Rawson, Argentina
- **Nationality:** Argentine
- **Appointed:** 2024

## Coaching Career
Calderón is a veteran coach with extensive experience across the Middle East and Africa:
- Argentina National Team (assistant)
- Saudi Arabia National Team
- Oman National Team
- Bahrain National Team
- Al-Hilal (Saudi Arabia)
- Haiti National Team (2024-present)

## Tactical Philosophy
- **Defensive Organization:** Structured 4-4-2 or 4-3-3
- **Counter-Attacking:** Uses Haiti's pace on the break
- **Physicality:** Leverages the athletic gifts of the squad
- **Set Pieces:** Well-drilled routines
