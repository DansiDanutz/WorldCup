### Playing Career

Solbakken was a midfielder who earned 58 caps for Norway and played at the 1998 World Cup. His playing career ended prematurely due to a heart attack in 2001.

### Tactical Approach

Solbakken has built Norway around their two superstars:
- **Star-centric system**: Everything designed to maximize Haaland and Odegaard
- **Flexible formations**: Switches between systems based on opponents
- **Youth integration**: Gives chances to Norway's emerging talents
- **Direct approach**: Gets the ball to Haaland as quickly as possible
- **Defensive pragmatism**: Accepts sitting deep against superior teams
