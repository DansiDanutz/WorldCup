## Squad Analysis

### Strengths
- **Erling Haaland**: The most prolific striker in world football
- **Martin Odegaard**: World-class playmaker and captain
- **Sander Berge**: Physically dominant midfielder
- **Counter-attacking threat**: Haaland's pace on the break is devastating
- **Team spirit**: First World Cup in 28 years creates unity

### Weaknesses
- **Over-reliance on Haaland**: If he's marked out, goals dry up
- **Defensive quality**: No elite center-backs at top clubs
- **Goalkeeper**: Nyland is solid but not world-class
- **Full-back depth**: Limited options at left-back
- **Tournament inexperience**: Most players have never played at a World Cup
