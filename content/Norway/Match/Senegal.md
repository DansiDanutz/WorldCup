# Norway vs Senegal — Group I, Matchday 2

**Date:** June 17, 2026 | **Tournament:** FIFA World Cup 2026

---

## Head-to-Head History

Norway and Senegal have never met in an official FIFA match.

---

## The Storyline: The Swing Match

With France likely topping Group I, this match could determine who joins them in the knockout rounds. Norway's star power (Haaland, Odegaard) against Senegal's collective strength (Koulibaly, Mane, Nicolas Jackson).

Senegal's 2002 World Cup quarter-final run showed what African teams can achieve on the big stage. Their 2022 Round of 16 appearance proved it wasn't a fluke. Against Norway, they face a team with two genuine superstars but less depth.

The tactical contrast is stark: Norway's direct, Haaland-centric attacking against Senegal's organized defense and rapid counter-attacking. Kalidou Koulibaly vs Erling Haaland is a heavyweight collision.

---

## Key Player Matchups

### Erling Haaland vs Kalidou Koulibaly
Haaland's movement against Koulibaly's defensive leadership. The veteran against the striker. Physical battle of the highest order.

### Martin Odegaard vs Lamine Camara
Odegaard's creativity against Camara's emerging midfield talent. If Odegaard dictates, Norway dominates.

### Sadio Mane vs Norway's Right Side
Mane's experience and finishing against Norway's defense. Even in his 30s, Mane is dangerous on the big stage.

### Nicolas Jackson vs Norway's Center-Backs
Jackson's pace and pressing against Norway's defensive organization. The Chelsea striker can exploit any hesitation.

---

## Tactical Preview

**Norway (4-2-3-1):** Direct to Haaland, create through Odegaard. Counter-attacking threat with Nusa's pace. Set pieces are important.

**Senegal (4-3-3):** Organized defense led by Koulibaly. Counter-attacks through Mane and Jackson. Senegal's collective discipline matches Norway's individual brilliance.

---

## X-Factor

**Kalidou Koulibaly's leadership.** The Senegalese captain has faced elite strikers his entire career. If he can organize the defense and contain Haaland, Senegal gains control of this match.
