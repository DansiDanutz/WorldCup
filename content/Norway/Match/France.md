# Norway vs France — Group I, Matchday 1

**Date:** June 12, 2026 | **Tournament:** FIFA World Cup 2026

---

## Head-to-Head History

Norway famously beat Brazil in the 1998 World Cup but has limited competitive history with France. They have met in qualifying campaigns and friendlies, with France generally holding the upper hand.

---

## The Storyline: Haaland vs the World

Erling Haaland has scored against virtually every elite defense in club football. Now, on the World Cup stage, he faces France — the nation that produced the 2022 World Cup final and boasts Mbappe, Dembele, and Tchouameni. It's a striker's dream and a defender's nightmare.

Norway's return to the World Cup after a 28-year absence is powered by two superstars: Haaland's goals and Martin Odegaard's creativity. Supporting them are Premier League-quality players like Sander Berge and Antonio Nusa. This is Norway's most talented team in a generation.

But France is France. The defending finalists have depth, experience, and tournament pedigree that Norway cannot match. The question isn't whether France is better — it's whether Haaland and Odegaard can bridge the gap through sheer individual brilliance.

---

## Key Player Matchups

### Erling Haaland vs William Saliba
Haaland's movement and finishing against Saliba's defensive class. These two face each other in the Premier League — now it's on the World Cup stage.

### Martin Odegaard vs Aurelien Tchouameni
Odegaard's creativity against Tchouameni's midfield dominance. If Odegaard finds space, Norway can create chances.

### Antonio Nusa vs Ousmane Dembele
Nusa's emerging talent against Dembele's established brilliance. The wide areas will feature scintillating pace.

### Sander Berge vs Kylian Mbappe (indirect)
Berge's midfield presence trying to stop the supply to Mbappe. Norway must cut off the service.

---

## Tactical Preview

**Norway (4-2-3-1):** Direct and counter-attacking. Everything flows through getting the ball to Haaland. Odegaard creates, Haaland finishes. Set pieces are a weapon with Norway's height.

**France (4-3-3):** Possession dominance with Mbappe's explosive pace on the counter. Tchouameni controls midfield. France can hurt you from any angle.

---

## X-Factor

**Erling Haaland's World Cup debut.** The most feared striker in world football making his World Cup bow against one of the tournament favorites. If Haaland scores, Norway believes. If he's contained, France's superiority tells.
