## Squad Analysis

### Strengths
- **Mitoma and Doan**: Elite wingers who terrorize full-backs
- **Endo**: Liverpool's midfield anchor brings Premier League quality
- **Tomiyasu**: Arsenal defender provides versatility and defensive solidity
- **Team cohesion**: Core group has played together for years
- **Tactical discipline**: Rarely makes defensive mistakes

### Weaknesses
- **Goalkeeper uncertainty**: Suzuki is talented but inexperienced
- **Striker consistency**: No elite No. 9 who guarantees 20+ goals
- **Physicality**: Can be overpowered by bigger European teams
- **Age in defense**: Yoshida (37) and Nagatomo (39) are past their peaks
