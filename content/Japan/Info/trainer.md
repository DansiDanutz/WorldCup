### Tactical Approach

Moriyasu has transformed Japan into a possession-based team that can compete with European powerhouses:
- **Possession football**: Averages 55%+ possession against top teams
- **High pressing**: Wins the ball high up the pitch
- **Flexibility**: Switches between 4-2-3-1 and 4-4-2 as needed
- **Youth trust**: Gives opportunities to young European-based players
- **Mental toughness**: Instilled belief that Japan can beat anyone
