### Skills and Strengths

Kaoru Mitoma is one of the Premier League's most exciting wingers, famous for his dribbling ability and late-game heroics.

**Key strengths:**
- **Dribbling**: Beats defenders with explosive acceleration and close control
- **1v1 ability**: Among the best in the Premier League at taking on full-backs
- **Finishing**: Scores crucial late goals; clutch performer
- **Work rate**: Defends diligently and tracks back
- **Crossing**: Delivers dangerous balls from the left flank
- **Calmness**: Rarely panics in the penalty area
