### Skills and Strengths

Ritsu Doan is Japan's right-wing dynamo, a direct dribbler who loves to cut inside and shoot.

**Key strengths:**

- **Dribbling**: Direct runner who takes on defenders
- **Finishing**: Scored crucial goals at the 2022 World Cup
- **Work rate**: Defends diligently and covers ground
- **Big-game mentality**: Scored against Germany and Spain in 2022
- **Set pieces**: Dangerous from free-kicks and corners
