### Skills and Strengths

- **Dribbling**: Exceptional close control and ability to beat defenders one-on-one
- **Vision**: Creative playmaker who sees passes others miss
- **Set pieces**: Excellent free kick taker
- **Versatility**: Can play on either wing, as a number 10, or as a false nine
- **Low center of gravity**: Uses his small frame (5'7") to his advantage, turning quickly in tight spaces

### What Others Say

### Fun Facts
1. **Barcelona youth product**: Kubo joined **FC Barcelona's famed La Masia academy** at age 10, but had to leave when FIFA banned him from playing in official matches due to transfer regulations regarding minors. He returned to Japan and rebuilt his career
2. He was the **youngest Japanese player to appear in La Liga** at 18 years old, and his transfer to Real Madrid at age 18 generated massive excitement in Japan
