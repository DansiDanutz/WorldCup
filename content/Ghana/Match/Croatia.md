# Ghana vs Croatia — Group L, Matchday 2

**Date:** June 17, 2026 | **Tournament:** FIFA World Cup 2026

---

## Head-to-Head History

Ghana and Croatia have **never met** in an official FIFA match. This is their first encounter at any level.

---

## The Storyline: The Midfield Battle

This match could be decided in the center of the pitch. Croatia's midfield — built around Luka Modric's genius and Mateo Kovacic's ball retention — against Ghana's engine room of Thomas Partey and Jordan Ayew's tireless pressing.

For Ghana, this is likely the pivotal match of the group. England may be too strong, Panama must be beaten — but Croatia is the swing match. A result here (draw or win) puts Ghana in contention for the knockout rounds.

The challenge is enormous. Croatia has reached the knockout stages in every World Cup they've qualified for since 1998. Their tournament mentality is proven. But Ghana's Black Stars have their own World Cup pedigree — quarter-finalists in 2010, with a team that nearly made the semi-finals.

## Key Player Matchups

### Thomas Partey vs Luka Modric
Partey's power against Modric's poetry. The Arsenal midfielder must disrupt Modric's rhythm — if the Croatian maestro has time on the ball, Ghana will chase shadows all match.

### Inaki Williams vs Josko Gvardiol
Williams' pace against Gvardiol's positioning. One counter-attack, one moment of acceleration, could produce a goal out of nothing.

### Antoine Semenyo vs Borna Sosa
Semenyo's direct running against Sosa's defensive discipline. The wide areas offer Ghana's best chance of creating chances.

### Jordan Ayew vs Andrej Kramaric
Ayew's leadership against Kramaric's finishing. Two experienced forwards who understand their roles perfectly.

**Croatia (4-3-3):** Patient possession through midfield control. Modric and Kovacic will try to dictate tempo. Kramaric provides the cutting edge. Croatia's tactical discipline makes them difficult to beat.
