## Skills and Strengths

- **Pace and Power:** Semenyo combines explosive speed with a powerful physique that makes him nearly impossible to stop in one-on-one situations. His driving runs from deep are a signature weapon.
- **Finishing:** A clinical finisher who has developed his goal-scoring consistently, culminating in 17 Premier League goals in the 2025/26 season.
- **Versatility:** Comfortable playing as a central striker, on the left wing, or as a second striker. This flexibility makes him invaluable for Queiroz's tactical setups.
- **Aerial Ability:** At 6'2" with strong leap timing, he is a threat in the air both offensively and defensively.
- **Hold-up Play:** Excellent at receiving the ball under pressure and bringing teammates into play, crucial for Ghana's counter-attacking style.

## Interesting Facts and Stories

### 1. From Non-League Football to Manchester City
Semenyo's journey to the top wasn't straightforward. He spent time on loan at non-league Bath City and Newport County while at Bristol City, playing in front of crowds of a few hundred. His rise from those humble surroundings to scoring the winning goal in an FA Cup Final for Manchester City is one of English football's great underdog stories. He reportedly still keeps in touch with his former Bath City teammates.

### 2. Choosing Ghana Over England
Despite being born and raised in London and playing for England at youth levels, Semenyo chose to represent Ghana — the country of his parents' heritage. He has spoken about how emotional it was to make his Black Stars debut, saying it fulfilled a promise he made to his father. His family in Ghana held a massive celebration in their village when he scored his first international goal.
