## Squad Analysis

### Strengths
- **Alphonso Davies**: World-class left-back who transforms the attack
- **Jonathan David**: Elite striker with 25+ goals for Lille
- **Marsch's pressing system**: Fits the squad's athletic profile
- **Home advantage**: Co-hosting provides familiarity and support
- **Forward depth**: David, Larin, and Buchanan offer goals

### Weaknesses
- **Goalkeeper aging**: Borjan is 37; questions about successor
- **Center-back quality**: No elite defenders at top European clubs
- **Inexperience**: Most players lack World Cup knockout experience
- **Pressure of hosting**: First-time host expectations
