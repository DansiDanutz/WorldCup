# Canada - FIFA World Cup 2026

## Group B: Canada, Belgium, Colombia, Qatar

---

## Team History

### The Maple Leafs

Canada's football history has been transformed in recent years. After a 36-year absence, they qualified for the 2022 World Cup and now co-host the 2026 tournament.

### World Cup Record

| Year | Host | Result | Key Moment |
|------|------|--------|------------|
| 1986 | Mexico | Group Stage | First World Cup; scored zero goals |
| 2022 | Qatar | Group Stage | Returned after 36 years; scored first-ever World Cup goal |

### The Breakthrough

Under John Herdman and now Jesse Marsch, Canada developed a golden generation featuring Alphonso Davies and Jonathan David. They won the 2025 CONCACAF Nations League.

---

## Playing Style

Canada plays an **intense, high-pressing 4-4-2 or 4-3-3** emphasizing:
- **Athleticism**: Among the fastest and fittest teams
- **Davies' wing play**: Alphonso Davies is the team's creative engine
- **Directness**: Attacks quickly with vertical passing
- **Physicality**: Strong, athletic players who dominate duels
- **Home advantage**: Co-hosting provides familiar conditions

---

## World Cup 2026 Prospects

As a co-host, Canada has automatic qualification and passionate home support. The goal is to reach the knockout stage for the first time ever.

**Key Question**: Can Davies and David deliver on home soil?
