### Skills and Strengths

Alphonso Davies is one of the most exciting players in world football, a generational talent who can dominate games from left-back.

**Key strengths:**
- **Pace**: Among the fastest players in professional football
- **Dribbling**: Takes on multiple defenders with ease
- **Crossing**: Delivers dangerous balls from the left flank
- **Defensive recovery**: Makes tackles he has no right to make
- **Versatility**: Equally devastating as a wing-back or winger
- **Big-game experience**: Won Champions League with Bayern Munich
