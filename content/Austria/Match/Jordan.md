# Austria vs Jordan — Group J, Matchday 3

**Date:** June 22, 2026 | **Tournament:** FIFA World Cup 2026

---

## Head-to-Head History

First-ever meeting between Austria and Jordan in official competition.

---

## The Storyline: The Final Group Curtain

Depending on earlier results, this match could determine advancement for one or both teams. Austria, as the stronger side on paper, will be expected to win — but Jordan has made a habit of defying expectations.

Jordan's 2024 Asian Cup run proved they can compete with technically superior teams through discipline, organization, and sheer will. Against Austria's pressing system, Jordan will need to be at their most structured and clinical on the counter.

For Austria, this is about professionalism. Rangnick will demand a ruthless, efficient performance that leaves nothing to chance.

---

## Key Player Matchups

### Marcel Sabitzer vs Noor Al-Rawabdeh
Sabitzer's experience and range of passing against Al-Rawabdeh's energy and positioning. Jordan's midfield general must keep Sabitzer quiet.

### Marko Arnautovic vs Yazan Al-Arab
Arnautovic's physicality against Al-Arab's defensive intelligence. A classic forward-vs-center-back duel.

### Musa Al-Taamari vs David Alaba
If Alaba pushes forward from the back, Al-Taamari will have space to exploit on the counter. One burst of pace could create a historic moment.

---

## Tactical Preview

**Austria (4-2-3-1):** Expected to control possession and territory. Rangnick's side will press Jordan's defensive block and look for openings through patient build-up play.

**Jordan (5-4-1):** Maximum defensive discipline. Every player behind the ball when out of possession. Al-Taamari and Al-Naimat will wait for their moment to strike on the counter.

---

## X-Factor

**Jordan's set-piece threat.** In a match where they may have limited possession, dead-ball situations are Jordan's most reliable path to goal. One free kick, one corner — that's all it takes for history.
