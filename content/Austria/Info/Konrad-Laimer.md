### Skills and Strengths

Konrad Laimer is the embodiment of Rangnick's pressing philosophy — a relentless, all-action midfielder whose stamina and intensity set the tone for Austria's game plan.

**Key strengths:**
- **Pressing intensity**: One of the most effective pressing midfielders in European football
- **Stamina**: Covers enormous ground; rarely substituted
- **Tackling**: Aggressive ball-winner with excellent timing
- **Versatility**: Equally effective at right-back or in central midfield
- **Ball progression**: Carries the ball effectively from deep positions
- **Team player**: Prioritizes collective success over individual statistics
