### Skills and Strengths

Marcel Sabitzer is the heartbeat of the Austrian midfield — a box-to-box dynamo who combines technical excellence with an eye for spectacular goals.

**Key strengths:**
- **Long-range shooting**: One of the best strike forces in European football from distance
- **Versatility**: Comfortable in any midfield position — deep, central, or advanced
- **Set pieces**: Excellent dead-ball delivery and finishing
- **Work rate**: Tireless pressing and defensive contribution
- **Leadership**: Former RB Leipzig captain, natural organizer
- **Big-game mentality**: Scores crucial goals in knockout matches
