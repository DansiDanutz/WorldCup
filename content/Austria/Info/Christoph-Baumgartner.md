### Skills and Strengths

Christoph Baumgartner is Austria's primary creative force — a nimble, intelligent playmaker who excels in finding space between the lines and delivering decisive passes.

**Key strengths:**
- **Spatial awareness**: Ghosts into pockets of space that defenders don't notice
- **Technical dribbling**: Low center of gravity allows him to weave through challenges
- **Vision**: Exceptional passing range for a player his age
- **Goal threat**: Contributes regularly with goals from midfield
- **Pressing ability**: Fits perfectly into Rangnick's gegenpressing system
- **Big-game performer**: Scored in major international tournaments
