### Skills and Strengths

Marko Arnautovic is Austria's all-time leading goalscorer and talisman — a powerful, skillful forward whose career has spanned nearly two decades at the highest level.

**Key strengths:**
- **Physicality**: Combines strength and agility like few other strikers
- **Technical skill**: Excellent close control and dribbling for a player his size
- **Finishing**: Clinical in the box with both feet and his head
- **Experience**: Has played in every major European league
- **Leadership**: The emotional heartbeat of the Austrian team
- **Charisma**: Lifts the entire team with his energy and passion

### Fun Facts

- Born in Vienna to Serbian parents; eligible for both Austria and Serbia
- His brother Daniel was also a professional footballer
- Famously substituted himself off during a Stoke City match — and was later defended by manager Mark Hughes
- Speaks German, Serbian, English, and Italian
- Known for his passionate celebrations, often involving acrobatic displays
- Played under Jose Mourinho at Inter Milan — twice (2010 and 2023)
