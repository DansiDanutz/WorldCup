### Skills and Strengths

David Alaba is one of the most versatile and technically gifted defenders of his generation. His ability to play anywhere across the backline or in midfield makes him invaluable to both club and country.

**Key strengths:**
- **Versatility**: World-class at left-back, centre-back, and defensive midfield
- **Technical quality**: Exceptional passer; can dictate tempo from deep positions
- **Set-piece specialist**: Deadly free-kick taker with remarkable accuracy
- **Leadership**: Captain of Austria, natural leader who organizes those around him
- **Experience**: Champions League winner with Bayern Munich (2013, 2020) and Real Madrid
- **Tactical intelligence**: Reads the game at an elite level, anticipates danger

### Fun Facts

- Born in Vienna to a Nigerian father and Filipino mother
- Holds the record as Austria's youngest-ever international debutant (17 years, 9 months)
- Speaks German, English, French, and Spanish fluently
- His father George was a rapper and DJ in Austria
- Named to the UEFA Team of the Year three consecutive times (2013-2015)
