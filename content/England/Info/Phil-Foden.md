### Skills and Strengths

Phil Foden is England's most technically gifted player, a magician with the ball at his feet who can unlock any defense.

**Key strengths:**
- **Dribbling**: Exceptional close control; weaves through tight spaces
- **Vision**: Sees passes others don't; creative playmaker
- **Finishing**: Both-footed finisher who scores from range and inside the box
- **Tactical intelligence**: Pep Guardiola's tactical prodigy; understands positional play deeply
- **Versatility**: Plays across the front line and midfield
- **Big-game player**: Scores and assists in crucial matches
