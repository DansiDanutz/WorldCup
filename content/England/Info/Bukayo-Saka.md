### Skills and Strengths

Bukayo Saka is England's most reliable wide attacker, combining dribbling skill with exceptional end product and work rate.

**Key strengths:**
- **One-on-one ability**: Consistently beats defenders on the wing
- **End product**: Excellent crossing and shooting; delivers goals and assists reliably
- **Work rate**: Tracks back diligently to help his full-back
- **Versatility**: Comfortable on either wing or at wing-back
- **Mentality**: Bounced back from Euro 2020 final penalty miss with remarkable resilience
- **Consistency**: Rarely has a bad game; dependable in big moments
