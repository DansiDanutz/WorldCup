### Skills and Strengths

Declan Rice is the anchor of England's midfield, combining defensive steel with progressive passing and surprising athleticism.

**Key strengths:**
- **Ball recovery**: Excellent tackling and interception instincts
- **Physical presence**: Powerful, athletic, and dominant in duels
- **Progressive passing**: Breaks lines with incisive forward passes
- **Leadership**: Vocal organizer who directs teammates around him
- **Stamina**: Covers huge distances every match
- **Aerial ability**: Strong in both defensive and attacking set-pieces
