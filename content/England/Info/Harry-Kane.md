### Skills and Strengths

Harry Kane is one of the greatest goalscorers in football history, combining lethal finishing with exceptional playmaking ability.

**Key strengths:**
- **Finishing**: Elite shot-striking with both feet; master of the half-chance
- **Movement**: Intelligent runs that exploit space between defenders
- **Link-up play**: Unusually creative for a striker; world-class passing range
- **Penalty taking**: Ice-cold from the spot
- **Aerial ability**: Strong in the air despite not being the tallest striker
- **Longevity**: Consistently scores 30+ goals per season across multiple leagues
