## Squad Analysis

### Strengths
- **World-class striker**: Harry Kane is one of the greatest goalscorers in football history
- **Midfield powerhouse**: Bellingham and Rice form arguably the best central midfield duo at the tournament
- **Attacking depth**: Saka, Foden, and Palmer offer creativity from multiple positions
- **Set-piece threat**: Maguire and Stones are dangerous from corners; Kane and Alexander-Arnold excel at direct free-kicks
- **Tournament experience**: Core group has played together since 2022

### Weaknesses
- **Left-back concerns**: Shaw and Chilwell have struggled with injuries
- **Goalkeeper questions**: Pickford is reliable but not world-class; lack of elite cover
- **Mental baggage**: History of collapsing under pressure in knockout stages
- **Right-back dilemma**: Alexander-Arnold's defensive limitations vs Walker's declining pace

### Key Tactical Setup
Tuchel is expected to deploy a **4-2-3-1** formation:
- Pickford in goal
- Walker, Stones, Guehi, Shaw across the back
- Rice and Bellingham as the double pivot
- Saka, Foden, and Palmer behind Kane
