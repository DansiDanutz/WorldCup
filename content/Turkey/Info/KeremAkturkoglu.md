## Interesting Facts

### Fact 1: The Most Controversial Transfer in Turkish Football
Kerem Akturkoglu committed what many Turkish fans consider the ultimate betrayal: he went from **Galatasaray to Fenerbahce** — the two fiercest rivals in Turkish football. But it wasn't a direct move. He first transferred to Benfica in Portugal for the 2024/25 season, then after just one year abroad, signed for Fenerbahce. The timing was particularly dramatic — he had made comments about his love for Galatasaray just days before the Fenerbahce transfer was announced. Galatasaray fans burned his shirt and he received death threats on social media. Despite the controversy, his performances on the pitch have been consistently excellent.

### Fact 2: Europa League Heroics Against Manchester United
Akturkoglu produced one of his finest performances in the Europa League, scoring in a thrilling 3-3 draw against Manchester United. He took two shots, both on target, and also completed three crosses in a dominant individual display. The match went viral in Turkey, with fans from both Galatasaray and Fenerbahce (temporarily) united in appreciation of his talent. His ability to perform on the biggest European stage convinced Fenerbahce to make him their marquee signing.
