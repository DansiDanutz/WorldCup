## Playing Style

Al-Shahrani is an experienced left-back known for his defensive reliability and attacking contributions. A mainstay of the national team for over a decade, he provides balance on the left side with intelligent overlapping runs and solid defensive work.

## Key Skills
- **Defensive Reliability:** Consistent and rarely beaten one-on-one
- **Crossing:** Accurate delivery from the left flank
- **Experience:** Over a decade of national team service
- **Stamina:** Maintains high energy levels throughout matches
- **Tactical Discipline:** Follows coach instructions to the letter
