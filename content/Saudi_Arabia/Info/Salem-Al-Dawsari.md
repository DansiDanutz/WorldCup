# Salem Al-Dawsari

## Profile
- **Full Name:** Salem Mohammed Al-Dawsari
- **Nickname:** "Salem"
- **Position:** Winger / Forward
- **Date of Birth:** August 19, 1991
- **Place of Birth:** Riyadh, Saudi Arabia
- **Height:** 1.73 m (5 ft 8 in)
- **Current Club:** Al-Hilal (Saudi Pro League)
- **Nationality:** Saudi Arabian

## Playing Style

Al-Dawsari is Saudi Arabia's talisman and most gifted attacker. A dynamic winger with explosive pace and exceptional dribbling, he is the player who makes the Green Falcons tick. His stunning goal against Argentina at the 2022 World Cup announced him to the global audience.

## Key Skills
- **Dribbling:** Elusive close control and trickery on the wing
- **Shooting:** Powerful shot from distance, capable of spectacular goals
- **Pace:** Quick acceleration to beat defenders
- **Set Pieces:** Reliable free-kick and corner taker
- **Leadership:** Senior figure who leads by example
