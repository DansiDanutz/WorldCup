# Saudi Arabia - 2026 World Cup Squad

## Squad Overview

Head coach **Georgios Donis** selected a squad almost entirely based in the Saudi Pro League, with **Saud Abdulhamid (Lens, France)** the only overseas-based player. Captain **Salem Al-Dawsari** leads a squad that blends World Cup experience with emerging youth talent.

## Notable Facts

- **Nawaf Al Aqidi** made stoppage-time saves in the final qualifying match that sealed qualification
- The squad has exceptional team chemistry with 25 of 26 players from the Saudi Pro League
