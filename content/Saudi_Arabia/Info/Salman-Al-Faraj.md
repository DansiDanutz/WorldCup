## Playing Style

Al-Faraj is Saudi Arabia's midfield metronome — a calm, composed passer who controls the tempo of play from deep positions. His experience and vision make him the tactical brain of the team, distributing the ball efficiently and shielding the defense.

## Key Skills
- **Passing:** Accurate over all distances, rarely wastes possession
- **Game Management:** Knows when to speed up and slow down play
- **Set Pieces:** Excellent free-kick and penalty taker
- **Leadership:** Former national team captain, vocal on the pitch
- **Experience:** Over 15 years of professional football at the highest level
