# Mohammed Al-Owais

## Profile
- **Full Name:** Mohammed Ibrahim Al-Owais
- **Nickname:** "Owais"
- **Position:** Goalkeeper
- **Date of Birth:** October 10, 1991
- **Place of Birth:** Riyadh, Saudi Arabia
- **Height:** 1.85 m (6 ft 1 in)
- **Current Club:** Al-Hilal (Saudi Pro League)
- **Nationality:** Saudi Arabian

## Playing Style

Al-Owais is a commanding goalkeeper whose shot-stopping heroics were crucial to Saudi Arabia's famous 2022 World Cup victory over Argentina. He combines good reflexes with improved distribution and commands his penalty area with authority.

## Key Skills
- **Shot Stopping:** Outstanding reflexes, particularly from close range
- **Commanding Presence:** Assertive in coming for crosses and set pieces
- **Distribution:** Improved ball-playing ability for modern tactical systems
- **Penalty Area Control:** Organizes his defense effectively
- **Big-Game Temperament:** Produced one of the great World Cup goalkeeper performances vs Argentina
