# Hassan Al-Haydos

## Profile
- **Full Name:** Hassan Khalid Al-Haydos
- **Nickname:** "Hassan"
- **Position:** Forward / Winger
- **Date of Birth:** December 11, 1990
- **Place of Birth:** Doha, Qatar
- **Height:** 1.73 m (5 ft 8 in)
- **Current Club:** Al-Sadd (Qatar Stars League)
- **Nationality:** Qatari
- **Captain:** Yes

## Playing Style

Al-Haydos is Qatar's long-serving captain and most capped player. His experience, leadership, and technical quality make him an irreplaceable figure despite being in the twilight of his career.

## Key Skills
- **Leadership:** Inspirational captain for club and country
- **Technical Ability:** Excellent close control and passing
- **Experience:** 160+ international caps
- **Versatility:** Can play across the front line
- **Set Pieces:** Reliable from free kicks and corners

## International Career
- **Qatar Caps:** 160+ (all-time leader)
- **International Goals:** 30+
- **Major Tournaments:** 2019 Asian Cup (champion), 2022 World Cup, 2026 World Cup

## Fun Facts
- The most capped player in Qatar's history
- Has spent his entire career at Al-Sadd
- One of the first graduates of the Aspire Academy
