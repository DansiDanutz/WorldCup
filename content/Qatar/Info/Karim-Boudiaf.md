### Skills and Strengths

Karim Boudiaf is Qatar's midfield anchor — a tough-tackling, experienced player who provides the defensive foundation for the team's counter-attacking style.

**Key strengths:**
- **Tackling**: Tenacious ball-winner in the center of the pitch
- **Positioning**: Excellent reading of the game; fills spaces effectively
- **Experience**: 130+ caps and counting
- **Leadership**: Organizes the midfield and communicates constantly
- **Passing**: Reliable distributor who keeps possession moving
