# Tintín Márquez — Head Coach

## Profile
- **Full Name:** Alejandro "Tintín" Márquez
- **Date of Birth:** October 7, 1964
- **Place of Birth:** Barcelona, Spain
- **Nationality:** Spanish
- **Appointed:** 2024

## Coaching Career
Márquez has extensive experience in Spanish and Qatari football:
- Espanyol (various roles)
- Qatar National Team (assistant)
- Al-Wakrah (Qatar Stars League)
- Qatar National Team (2024-present)

## Tactical Philosophy
- **Possession Football:** Emphasizes ball retention and patient build-up
- **Spanish Influence:** Tiki-taka principles adapted to Qatari players
- **Technical Development:** Focuses on improving individual technique
- **Pressing:** Organized pressing in midfield areas
