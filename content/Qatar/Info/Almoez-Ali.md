## Playing Style

Ali is Qatar's primary goalscorer — a clinical finisher with intelligent movement and a knack for being in the right place at the right time. He won the Golden Boot at the 2019 Asian Cup with 9 goals.

## Key Skills
- **Finishing:** Clinical in front of goal with both feet
- **Movement:** Intelligent runs that find space in the box
- **Aerial Ability:** Good header of the ball
- **Link-Up Play:** Holds the ball and brings others into play
- **Goal Scoring Record:** Consistent scorer at both club and international level
