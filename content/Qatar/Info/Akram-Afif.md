# Akram Afif

## Profile
- **Full Name:** Akram Hassan Afif
- **Nickname:** "Akram"
- **Position:** Winger / Forward
- **Date of Birth:** November 18, 1996
- **Place of Birth:** Doha, Qatar
- **Height:** 1.75 m (5 ft 9 in)
- **Current Club:** Al-Sadd (Qatar Stars League)
- **Nationality:** Qatari / Yemeni

## Playing Style

Afif is Qatar's most gifted player — a creative winger with exceptional vision and technical ability. He was the star of Qatar's 2019 Asian Cup triumph and remains the team's most important creative force.

## Key Skills
- **Vision:** Exceptional passing range from wide positions
- **Technical Ability:** Outstanding close control and dribbling
- **Set Pieces:** Accurate free-kick and corner delivery
- **Versatility:** Can play as a winger, #10, or false 9
- **Big-Game Player:** Delivers in crucial moments
