# Abdelkarim Hassan

## Profile
- **Full Name:** Abdelkarim Hassan Fadlallah
- **Position:** Left-Back
- **Date of Birth:** August 28, 1993
- **Place of Birth:** Doha, Qatar
- **Height:** 1.78 m (5 ft 10 in)
- **Current Club:** Al-Sadd (Qatar Stars League)
- **Nationality:** Qatari / Sudanese

## Playing Style

Hassan is an attacking left-back known for his overlapping runs and crossing ability. One of the most experienced players in the squad, he provides width and energy down the left flank.

## Key Skills
- **Attacking Runs:** Constant threat from left-back
- **Crossing:** Accurate delivery from wide positions
- **Experience:** 80+ international caps
- **Stamina:** Maintains high energy throughout matches
- **Free Kicks:** Powerful left-foot shot

## International Career
