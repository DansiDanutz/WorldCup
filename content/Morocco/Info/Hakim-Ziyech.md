# Hakim Ziyech

## Profile
- **Full Name:** Hakim Ziyech
- **Nickname:** "The Wizard"
- **Position:** Attacking Midfielder / Winger
- **Date of Birth:** March 19, 1993
- **Place of Birth:** Dronten, Netherlands
- **Height:** 1.80 m (5 ft 11 in)
- **Current Club:** Galatasaray (Turkish Süper Lig)
- **Nationality:** Moroccan / Dutch

## Playing Style

Ziyech is a creative genius with a wand of a left foot. His passing range, vision, and ability to score spectacular goals from distance make him one of football's most entertaining players. A player who sees angles and executes passes that others wouldn't even attempt.

## Key Skills
- **Left Foot:** One of the best left feet in football — passing, shooting, crossing
- **Vision:** Sees and executes passes that seem impossible
- **Long-Range Shooting:** Scores spectacular goals from 25+ yards
- **Set Pieces:** Pinpoint free-kick delivery
- **Creativity:** The player who makes Morocco's attack function
