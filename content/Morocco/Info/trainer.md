# Walid Regragui — Head Coach

## Profile
- **Full Name:** Walid Regragui
- **Nickname:** "Regragui"
- **Position:** Former Right-Back
- **Date of Birth:** September 23, 1975
- **Place of Birth:** Corbeil-Essonnes, France
- **Nationality:** Moroccan
- **Appointed:** 2022
- **Coaching License:** FIFA Pro License

## Playing Career
Played as a right-back for Toulouse, Ajaccio, and Racing Santander, earning 45 caps for Morocco.

## Coaching Career

| Period | Role | Team |
|--------|------|------|
| 2014-2020 | Head Coach | Fath Union Sport (Morocco) |
| 2020-2021 | Head Coach | Al-Duhail (Qatar) |
| 2021-2022 | Head Coach | Wydad AC (Morocco) |
| 2022-Present | Head Coach | Morocco National Team |

## Tactical Philosophy

Regragui is the architect of Morocco's historic 2022 World Cup semifinal run — the first African team to achieve this. His system is built on:

- **Defensive Solidity:** Organized 4-1-4-1 or 4-2-3-1 low block
- **Counter-Attacking Speed:** Rapid transitions using pace of Hakimi, Ziyech
- **Set Piece Excellence:** Both attacking and defending
- **Team Spirit:** Incredible unity and fighting spirit
- **Pragmatism:** Adapts tactics to opposition strength

## Achievements
- 2022 FIFA World Cup: 4th place (best ever by an African nation)
- 2022 CAF Award for Best Coach
- Won CAF Champions League with Wydad AC (2022)
- Moroccan League champion with FUS and Wydad
