## Playing Style

El Khannouss is one of Europe's most exciting young playmakers. A gifted #10 with exceptional vision, close control, and creativity, he has already established himself as a Premier League regular. His ability to find space between the lines and play decisive passes makes him Morocco's future.

## Key Skills

- **Creativity:** Regularly tops the chance-creation charts
- **Composure:** Plays with maturity far beyond his age
- **Versatility:** Can play as a #10, #8, or on either wing
