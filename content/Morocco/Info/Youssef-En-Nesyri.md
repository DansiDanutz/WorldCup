# Youssef En-Nesyri

## Profile
- **Full Name:** Youssef En-Nesyri
- **Nickname:** "En-Nesyri"
- **Position:** Striker
- **Date of Birth:** June 1, 1997
- **Place of Birth:** Fès, Morocco
- **Height:** 1.90 m (6 ft 3 in)
- **Current Club:** Fenerbahçe (Turkish Süper Lig)
- **Nationality:** Moroccan

## Playing Style

En-Nesyri is a powerful target man with exceptional aerial ability and improving finishing. His towering presence in the box makes him a constant threat from crosses and set pieces. His pace and power make him difficult for any defender to handle.

## Key Skills
- **Aerial Ability:** Outstanding header of the ball — scored multiple World Cup goals with his head
- **Physicality:** Strong, athletic, and difficult to push off the ball
- **Pace:** Surprisingly quick for a player of his size
- **Finishing:** Clinical inside the penalty area
- **Work Rate:** Presses from the front and tracks back when needed

- Scored Morocco's quarterfinal goal vs Portugal at the 2022 World Cup with a towering header
- His goal celebration — pointing to the sky — is dedicated to his late grandmother

- Came through Mohammed VI Football Academy, Morocco's elite development center
