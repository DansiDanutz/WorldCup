## Playing Style

Amrabat is a tireless defensive midfielder who became a global star during Morocco's 2022 World Cup semifinal run. His relentless pressing, ball-winning ability, and incredible stamina make him the engine of Morocco's midfield. He covers every blade of grass.

## Key Skills
- **Ball Winning:** Exceptional at winning possession through tackles and interceptions
- **Stamina:** Runs for 90+ minutes without any drop in intensity
- **Pressing:** Relentless pressing that disrupts opposition build-up
- **Physicality:** Strong in challenges and difficult to bypass
- **Passing:** Efficient distributor who keeps play moving
