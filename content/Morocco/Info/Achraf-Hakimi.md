# Achraf Hakimi

## Profile
- **Full Name:** Achraf Hakimi Mouh
- **Nickname:** "Achraf"
- **Position:** Right-Back
- **Date of Birth:** November 4, 1998
- **Place of Birth:** Madrid, Spain
- **Height:** 1.81 m (5 ft 11 in)
- **Current Club:** Paris Saint-Germain (Ligue 1)
- **Nationality:** Moroccan

## Playing Style

Hakimi is arguably the best right-back in world football — a devastating attacking force who also defends with intelligence. His pace is world-class, his crossing pinpoint, and his finishing from distance is a genuine weapon. He is Morocco's most important player.

## Key Skills
- **Pace:** One of the fastest players in professional football
- **Attacking Threat:** Contributes double-digit goals and assists from right-back
- **Crossing:** Accurate whipped deliveries into the box
- **Free Kicks:** Powerful, curling set-piece specialist
- **Versatility:** Can play RB, RWB, or even as a winger
