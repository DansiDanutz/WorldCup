## Career Journey

- **Kashima Antlers** (2017–2021) — J-League, won Emperor's Cup
- **Vissel Kobe** (2021–2023) — J-League champion 2023
- **Machida Zelvia** (2024–present) — J-League

### J-League Specialist
Oh has spent his entire professional career in Japan's J-League, becoming one of the most consistent Korean strikers in Japanese football. His 2023 season with Vissel Kobe helped the club win its first-ever J-League title.

---

## Playing Style

- **Target man**: Strong hold-up play, brings others into the game
- **Aerial threat**: Good in the air, useful on set pieces
- **Link-up play**: Intelligent movement, creates space for Son and Lee
- **Work rate**: Presses from the front, fits Hong's high-energy system

## Fun Facts

- One of the few Korean internationals to have played exclusively in Japan
- His height (6'1") makes him one of Korea's tallest attacking options
- Known as a super-sub for the national team
- Speaks fluent Japanese after 8+ years in the J-League
