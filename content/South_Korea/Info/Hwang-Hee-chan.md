## Career Journey

- **Red Bull Salzburg** (2016–2020) — Austrian Bundesliga champion, Europa League standout
- **RB Leipzig** (2020–2021) — Bundesliga, Champions League
- **Wolverhampton** (2021–present) — Premier League cult hero

### The Wolves Icon
Hwang became a fan favorite at Wolves for his relentless energy and clutch goals. His 2022 World Cup goal against Portugal — a 91st-minute winner that sent South Korea through — cemented his status as a national hero.

---

## Playing Style

- **Energy**: Non-stop running, presses from the front relentlessly
- **Physicality**: Strong in challenges, doesn't get knocked off the ball
- **Versatility**: Can play striker, winger, or attacking midfield
- **Clutch**: Scores important goals in big moments
- **Team player**: Willing to sacrifice personal stats for the team

## Fun Facts
