## Career Journey

- **Jeonbuk Hyundai** (2017–2019) — K League champion
- **Beijing Guoan** (2019–2021) — Chinese Super League
- **Fenerbahce** (2021–2022) — Turkish Super Lig standout
- **Napoli** (2022–2023) — Serie A champion, named Serie A Best Defender
- **Bayern Munich** (2023–present) — Bundesliga champion, Champions League

### The Napoli Season
Kim's 2022/23 season at Napoli was one of the greatest individual defensive campaigns in Serie A history. He was the backbone of Napoli's first Scudetto in 33 years, earning him the nickname "The Monster" from Italian fans.

---

## Playing Style

- **Physical dominance**: 6'3", incredibly strong in challenges
- **Recovery pace**: Surprisingly fast for his size, excellent last-man defender
- **Aerial ability**: Dominant in both boxes from set pieces
- **Ball-playing**: Comfortable bringing the ball out from the back
- **Aggressive**: Front-foot defender who steps up to intercept
