## Career Journey

- **Valencia** (2018–2021) — La Liga debut at 17, youngest Korean to play in La Liga
- **Mallorca** (2021–2023) — Breakout season, 6 goals and 7 assists in La Liga
- **Paris Saint-Germain** (2023–present) — Ligue 1 champion, playing alongside Dembele and Barcola

### Golden Boy
Lee won the **Golden Ball at the 2019 FIFA U-20 World Cup**, leading South Korea to the final. His technical ability and vision have drawn comparisons to David Silva and Philippe Coutinho.

---

## Playing Style

- **Technical mastery**: Exceptional close control and dribbling
- **Vision**: Sees passes that others can't, creative playmaker
- **Set pieces**: Deadly free-kick and corner delivery
- **Composure**: Plays with maturity well beyond his 23 years
- **Flexibility**: Can play as 10, 8, or on either wing

---

## Fun Facts

- Was born in Incheon but moved to Spain at age 10 to join Valencia's academy
- Speaks fluent Spanish and Korean
- Won the Golden Ball at 2019 U-20 World Cup (previous winners include Messi and Pogba)
- His PSG transfer made him the most expensive Korean player ever
- Known for his trademark "finger to lips" celebration
