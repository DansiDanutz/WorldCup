### Playing Career

Hong Myung-bo was a legendary South Korean defender who captained the 2002 World Cup semi-finalist team. He won the Bronze Ball as the tournament's third-best player.

### Tactical Approach

Hong emphasizes defensive organization and quick counter-attacks:
- **Solid defensive block**: Prioritizes shape and discipline
- **Son as focal point**: Builds the attack around Son Heung-min
- **Youth integration**: Gives chances to Europe-based youngsters
- **Mental toughness**: Instills the never-give-up spirit of 2002
