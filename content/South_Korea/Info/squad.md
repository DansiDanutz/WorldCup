## Squad Analysis

### Strengths
- **Son Heung-min**: World-class forward; can win matches single-handedly
- **Kim Min-jae**: Bayern Munich center-back; elite defender
- **Lee Kang-in**: PSG playmaker with enormous potential
- **Counter-attacking speed**: Devastating on the break
- **Fighting spirit**: Never-say-die attitude

### Weaknesses
- **Over-reliance on Son**: If he's marked out, creativity drops
- **Goalkeeper concerns**: No elite goalkeeper in the squad
- **Aging defense**: Kim Young-gwon and Jung Woo-young are 35+
- **Striker depth**: Cho Gue-sung is good but not world-class
