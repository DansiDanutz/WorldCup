## Career Journey

- **Hamburger SV** (2010–2013) — Bundesliga breakthrough
- **Bayer Leverkusen** (2013–2015) — 29 goals in 87 appearances
- **Tottenham Hotspur** (2015–2025) — 173 goals in 454 appearances, Premier League Golden Boot 2021/22 (23 goals, no penalties)
- **LAFC** (2025–present) — MLS, first Asian Designated Player

### Premier League Legend
Son is the **highest-scoring Asian player in Premier League history** and one of only 33 players to score 100+ Premier League goals. His 2021/22 Golden Boot — shared with Mohamed Salah — was achieved without a single penalty.

---

## Playing Style

- **Two-footed**: Genuinely ambidextrous, can shoot and pass with both feet
- **Pace**: Explosive acceleration, devastating on the break
- **Finishing**: Clinical in one-on-one situations
- **Work rate**: Exceptional pressing and tracking back
- **Leaders**: South Korea captain, carries the team emotionally
