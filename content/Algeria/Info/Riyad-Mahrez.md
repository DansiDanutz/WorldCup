## Playing Style

Mahrez is Algeria's greatest ever player — a magician on the ball whose trickery, vision, and finishing have enchanted fans from Leicester to Manchester and beyond. His signature move, cutting inside from the right to curl a left-footed shot, is one of football's most recognizable weapons.

## Key Skills
- **Dribbling:** Mesmeric close control and feints that bewildered Premier League defenders for a decade
- **Left Foot:** Devastating curling shots and passes from the right flank
- **Vision:** Creates chances that seem impossible with weighted through balls
- **Free Kicks:** A genuine threat from 25 yards
- **Big-Game Player:** Scores in finals and decisive moments
