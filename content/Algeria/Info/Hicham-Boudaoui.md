## Profile
- **Full Name:** Hicham Boudaoui
- **Nickname:** "Hicham"
- **Position:** Central Midfielder
- **Date of Birth:** September 1, 1999
- **Place of Birth:** Béchar, Algeria
- **Height:** 1.75 m (5 ft 9 in)

## Playing Style

Boudaoui is an energetic, box-to-box midfielder who combines physicality with technical ability. His relentless pressing and intelligent positioning make him an effective ball-winner, while his passing range allows him to contribute to attacking play.

## Key Skills
- **Work Rate:** Covers every inch of the midfield
- **Pressing:** Intelligent pressing that wins the ball high up the pitch
- **Passing:** Efficient distribution that maintains team tempo
- **Tackling:** Strong in challenges and effective at intercepting
- **Versatility:** Can play defensive or central midfield
