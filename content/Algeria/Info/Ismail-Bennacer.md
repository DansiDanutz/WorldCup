# Ismaël Bennacer

## Profile
- **Full Name:** Ismaël Bennacer
- **Nickname:** "Isma"
- **Position:** Central Midfielder
- **Date of Birth:** December 1, 1997
- **Place of Birth:** Arles, France
- **Height:** 1.70 m (5 ft 7 in)
- **Current Club:** AC Milan (Serie A)
- **Nationality:** Algerian / French

## Playing Style

Bennacer is a technically superb midfielder who controls games with his passing and dribbling. Despite his small stature, he is remarkably effective in defensive midfield, using intelligence and anticipation to win the ball. His ball progression from deep is among the best in Serie A.

## Key Skills
- **Passing Accuracy:** Consistently above 90% in league matches
- **Dribbling:** Low center of gravity makes him nearly impossible to dispossess
- **Ball Progression:** Drives the team forward from deep positions
- **Press Resistance:** Calm and composed under intense pressure
- **Work Rate:** Covers every blade of grass in midfield
