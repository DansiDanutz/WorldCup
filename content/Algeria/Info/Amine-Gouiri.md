## Playing Style

Gouiri is a silky forward with exceptional technical ability and a lethal eye for goal. Comfortable playing across the front line, he combines dribbling flair with intelligent movement and clinical finishing. One of Algeria's most exciting young talents.

## Key Skills
- **Technical Ability:** Brilliant close control and trickery
- **Finishing:** Clinical with both feet and head
- **Movement:** Intelligent runs that create scoring opportunities
- **Versatility:** Can play as a #9, left winger, or second striker
- **Creativity:** Provides assists as well as goals
