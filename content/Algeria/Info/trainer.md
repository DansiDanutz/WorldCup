# Vladimir Petković — Head Coach

## Profile
- **Full Name:** Vladimir Petković
- **Date of Birth:** August 15, 1963
- **Nationality:** Bosnian-Swiss
- **Appointed:** 2024
- **Coaching License:** FIFA Pro License

## Coaching Career

| Period | Role | Team |
|--------|------|------|
| 2014-2021 | Head Coach | Switzerland National Team |
| 2012-2014 | Head Coach | S.S. Lazio (Serie A) |
| 2008-2012 | Head Coach | BSC Young Boys |
| 2005-2008 | Head Coach | FC Thun |
| 2024-Present | Head Coach | Algeria National Team |

## Tactical Philosophy

Petković favors a flexible 4-2-3-1 or 4-3-3 system that emphasizes:
- **Defensive organization:** Structured pressing from the front
- **Counter-attacking:** Rapid transitions using Algeria's pace on the wings
- **Set pieces:** Both offensive and defensive
- **Youth integration:** Blending experienced campaigners with emerging talent

## Achievements
- Led Switzerland to Euro 2020 quarterfinals (best result in 67 years)
- Won the Swiss Cup with Young Boys
- Multiple top-four finishes with Lazio in Serie A
