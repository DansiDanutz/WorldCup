# Algeria vs Austria — Group J, Matchday 2

**Date:** June 17, 2026 | **Tournament:** FIFA World Cup 2026

---

## Head-to-Head History

Algeria and Austria have **never met** in an official FIFA match. This is their first encounter at any level.

---

## The Storyline: The Battle for Survival

This is likely the match that decides second place in Group J. Both Algeria and Austria will expect to lose to Argentina and beat Jordan — making this head-to-head a virtual knockout match in the group stage.

The contrast in styles creates a fascinating tactical contest. Algeria's African flair and technical quality against Austria's European discipline and gegenpressing intensity. Petkovic vs Rangnick — two coaches with diametrically opposed philosophies.

For Algeria, this is the match they must win. For Austria, a draw might suffice depending on other results. The tension will be unbearable.

## Key Player Matchups

### Riyad Mahrez vs Konrad Laimer
Mahrez's creativity against Laimer's relentless pressing. If Laimer can shackle Mahrez, Algeria loses their primary creative outlet.

### Marcel Sabitzer vs Ismail Bennacer
The midfield engine room. Sabitzer's power and shooting against Bennacer's vision and control. This duel could decide the match.

### Hicham Boudaoui vs Christoph Baumgartner
Two dynamic midfielders who arrive late in the box. Whoever contributes more in transition will tip the balance.
