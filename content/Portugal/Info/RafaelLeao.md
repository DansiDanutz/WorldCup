## Skills and Strengths

- **Pace**: One of the fastest players in Serie A and world football -- his acceleration and top speed are devastating
- **Dribbling**: Exceptional one-on-one ability, comfortable beating defenders with skill or pure speed
- **Power and size**: At 1.88 m, he combines winger speed with striker size, making him almost impossible to stop at full stride
- **Left-wing threat**: Predominantly operates on the left flank, cutting inside onto his right foot or driving to the byline
- **Counter-attacking**: Perhaps the most dangerous counter-attacking winger in the tournament
- **Finishing improvement**: Has steadily improved his goal-scoring output each season at AC Milan
- **Creativity**: Not just a scorer but also a prolific assist provider with excellent crossing ability

## Interesting Facts

1. **He left Sporting CP in extraordinary circumstances**: In 2018, Leao unilaterally terminated his contract with Sporting CP after a violent incident where a group of fans attacked players and staff at the training ground. He moved to Lille on a free transfer, and Sporting received no fee. He was just 19 at the time. The incident was one of the darkest in Portuguese football history, but it launched Leao's international career.

2. **He scored one of the fastest goals in Serie A history**: On March 19, 2023, Leao scored against Sassuolo just 6.2 seconds into the match, one of the fastest goals ever recorded in Serie A. He received the kick-off, sprinted past three defenders, and slotted the ball past the goalkeeper before most fans had settled into their seats. The goal went viral worldwide and cemented his reputation as one of the most dangerous forwards in Europe.
