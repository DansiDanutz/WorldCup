## Skills and Strengths

- **Vision and passing**: Elite playmaker with the ability to find passes others do not see. Averages over 3 key passes per game
- **Set-piece delivery**: One of the best dead-ball specialists in world football, dangerous from free kicks and corners
- **Goal threat from midfield**: Consistently scores double-digit goals from midfield -- a rare combination of creativity and finishing
- **Work rate**: Presses relentlessly, tracks back, and leads by example with his energy
- **Leadership**: Captains Manchester United and is a vocal leader for Portugal
- **Long-range shooting**: Capable of spectacular goals from distance with either foot
- **Big-game mentality**: Tends to elevate his performance in crucial matches

## Interesting Facts

1. **He almost quit football as a teenager**: After trials at Real Madrid and Boavista did not work out, a young Bruno considered giving up football entirely. His family convinced him to accept an offer from Novara in Italy's third division, which ultimately launched his professional career. The Italian adventure transformed him from a struggling Portuguese youth player into a Serie A regular.

2. **His record-breaking Sporting CP season was historic**: In 2018-19, Bruno scored 33 goals and provided 18 assists from central midfield in all competitions -- numbers that shattered expectations for his position. His performances earned him a move to Manchester United for approximately 55 million euros, and he scored on his debut in a 0-4 win over Norwich City.
