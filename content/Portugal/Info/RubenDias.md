## Skills and Strengths

- **Defensive positioning**: Elite reading of the game, consistently in the right place to intercept or block
- **Leadership and communication**: Organizes the back line with constant verbal instructions -- a natural captain
- **Aerial dominance**: Strong in both defensive and offensive set pieces, wins the majority of headers
- **Composure on the ball**: Comfortable playing out from the back, fitting Pep Guardiola's system perfectly
- **One-on-one defending**: Rarely beaten in individual duels, combines strength with timing
- **Tactical intelligence**: Understands when to step out of the defensive line and when to hold position
- **Durability**: Consistently plays 40+ matches per season at the highest level

## Interesting Facts

1. **He won FWA Footballer of the Year in his first Premier League season**: The Football Writers' Association named Ruben Dias the best player in English football for the 2020-21 campaign, making him only the second defender to win the award in the 21st century (after John Terry in 2005). He beat out the likes of Kevin De Bruyne and Bruno Fernandes to the honor, a remarkable achievement for a player in his debut season at a new club.

2. **He comes from a family of footballers**: Ruben's older brother, Ivan Dias, was also a professional footballer who played as a centre-back for clubs in the Portuguese lower divisions. Ruben has credited his brother as a major influence on his career, saying that growing up competing against an older, stronger sibling taught him the defensive toughness that defines his game today.
