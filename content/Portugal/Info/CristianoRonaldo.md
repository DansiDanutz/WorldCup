## Skills and Strengths

- **Aerial ability**: One of the greatest headers of the ball in football history, with remarkable hang time and positioning
- **Finishing**: Clinical in front of goal with both feet, capable of scoring from any angle or distance
- **Athleticism**: Even at 41, maintains extraordinary physical conditioning and explosiveness
- **Free kicks**: Known for his knuckleball technique, especially during his Real Madrid prime
- **Positioning**: Elite off-the-ball movement, consistently finding space in the penalty area
- **Mentality**: Unmatched drive, self-belief, and competitive spirit. Has maintained elite performance for over two decades
- **Leadership**: Captain of Portugal, leads by example with his work ethic and determination
- **Adaptability**: Has evolved his game from a rapid winger at Manchester United to a lethal penalty-box striker in his later years

## Interesting Facts

1. **Named after Ronald Reagan**: Cristiano's father, Jose Dinis Aveiro, was a fan of US President Ronald Reagan and named his son after him. His second name, "Ronaldo," was chosen in honor of the actor-turned-president.

2. **Heart condition as a teenager**: At age 15, Ronaldo was diagnosed with a racing heart condition (tachycardia) that could have ended his career before it started. He underwent laser surgery to cauterize the affected area and was back training within days. The condition has never recurred.

3. **The CR7 brand began by accident**: The iconic "CR7" moniker was not his creation. It emerged when he joined Manchester United and was given the number 7 shirt (previously worn by George Best, Bryan Robson, Eric Cantona, and David Beckham). He initially wanted number 28 but Alex Ferguson insisted he take 7.

4. **He has scored 50+ goals after turning 40**: While most players are long retired at that age, Ronaldo continues to score at a remarkable rate for Al Nassr and Portugal, defying the conventional understanding of athletic longevity.
