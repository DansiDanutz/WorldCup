## Skills and Strengths

### Vision and Creativity
Wirtz possesses extraordinary vision for a player of his age. He sees passes that most players don't even contemplate -- through balls that split three defenders, reverse passes that unlock packed defenses, and disguised balls that deceive the entire opposition. His creativity is the foundation of Germany's attacking play.

### Dribbling and Ball Control
Despite his relatively small frame (1.76 m), Wirtz is a superb dribbler. His low center of gravity allows him to change direction rapidly, and his close control in tight spaces is exceptional. He thrives in crowded areas where other players would lose the ball.

### Finishing
Wirtz has developed into a genuine goal threat from midfield. He can score from distance, finish one-on-ones, and arrive late in the box to convert chances. His 10 goals in 39 Germany appearances demonstrate his ability to contribute end product alongside creativity.

### Press Resistance
One of Wirtz's most valuable qualities is his ability to receive the ball under pressure and retain possession. He rarely loses the ball in tight situations, using his body positioning and quick feet to shield the ball and find a pass.

### Set Pieces
Wirtz is a dangerous set-piece taker, capable of delivering precise corners and free kicks. His technique from dead-ball situations adds another dimension to Germany's attacking threat.

## Funny and Interesting Facts

### 1. The ACL That Changed Everything
In March 2022, Wirtz suffered a devastating ACL tear that sidelined him for over nine months. The injury occurred during a Europa League match, and at just 18 years old, many feared it could derail the career of Germany's most exciting prospect. What happened next was remarkable: Wirtz used the recovery period to study the game obsessively, watching hundreds of hours of match footage and analyzing his own performances. When he returned, he was not only physically stronger but tactically sharper. The injury, he later said, was "the best thing that could have happened to me" because it gave him perspective and time to develop his understanding of the game. The 2023/24 season that followed was the greatest in Bayer Leverkusen's history, with Wirtz as the creative hub. He then earned a move to Liverpool, where he has continued his upward trajectory.

### 2. The Record-Breaking Teenager
Wirtz was so talented that Bayer Leverkusen promoted him to the first team at age 17, making him the youngest player in the club's Bundesliga history. But the records didn't stop there: he became the first player ever to score five Bundesliga goals before turning 18, and the youngest to reach 50 Bundesliga appearances. His parents, who are both involved in football (his father is a youth coach), have been credited with keeping him grounded. Wirtz reportedly still lived at home during his early Leverkusen years, with his mother cooking meals to ensure he maintained a proper diet. Teammates joked that the "Wonderkid of Leverkusen" was the only Bundesliga star who had a curfew.

### Bonus Facts

- Wirtz's transfer to Liverpool in 2025 was one of the most expensive in the club's history, reportedly around 100 million euros.
- He scored on his Bundesliga debut as a 17-year-old, becoming Leverkusen's youngest ever goalscorer.
- His favorite position is as a number 10, operating in the space between the opposition's midfield and defense.
