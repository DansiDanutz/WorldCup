## Career Journey

- **Mamelodi Sundowns** (2014–2018) — CAF Champions League winner 2016
- **Brighton & Hove Albion** (2018–2021) — Signed but work permit issues led to loans
- **Union SG** (loan, Belgium) — Belgian Pro League standout
- **Club Brugge** (loan, Belgium) — Belgian champion
- **Al-Ahly** (2021–present) — CAF Champions League finalist, Egyptian Premier League

---

## Playing Style

- **Dribbling**: Electric close control, beats defenders one-on-one
- **Pace**: Explosive acceleration, devastating on the counter
- **Creativity**: Excellent vision and passing range
- **Versatility**: Can play on either wing or as a #10

## Fun Facts

- His transfer to Brighton was complicated by UK work permit rules, leading to three loan spells
- Was the first South African to play in the Belgian Pro League
- His father was also a professional footballer
- Known for his humility despite being South Africa's biggest star
