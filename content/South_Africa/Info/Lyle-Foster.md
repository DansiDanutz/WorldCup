---

## Playing Style

- **Hold-up play**: Strong with back to goal, brings others into play
- **Physicality**: Powerful runner, hard to knock off the ball
- **Link-up play**: Good combination play with teammates
- **Aerial threat**: Useful target man on set pieces

## Fun Facts

- One of the few South Africans playing in English football
- Has been open about mental health challenges, becoming an advocate
- His move to Burnley made him one of South Africa's most expensive exports
- Speaks fluent Portuguese after his time in Portugal
