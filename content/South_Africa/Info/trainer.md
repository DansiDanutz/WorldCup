## Coaching Career

- **Anderlecht** (Belgium) — Multiple Belgian league titles
- **Club Brugge** (Belgium) — Domestic success
- **Cameroon National Team** (2016–2018) — Won 2017 Africa Cup of Nations
- **South Africa National Team** (2021–present) — Qualified for 2026 World Cup, 2023 AFCON semifinals

### Africa Cup of Nations Champion
Broos is one of the few European coaches to win the AFCON, leading Cameroon to the 2017 title. His success in African football has made him one of the continent's most respected coaches.

## Tactical Approach

- **Defensive solidity**: Compact 4-5-1, prioritizes not conceding
