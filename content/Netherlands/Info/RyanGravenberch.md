## Interesting Facts

1. **He joined Ajax's academy at age 8 and was earmarked for greatness from the start**: Gravenberch was identified as a special talent from the moment he entered Ajax's famous De Toekomst academy. By age 16, he was already training with the first team, and he made his senior debut at 16 years and 130 days old, becoming the youngest player ever to appear for Ajax in the Eredivisie -- breaking a record that had stood since 1991. The entire Amsterdam football community knew he was destined for the top from an early age.

2. **His career was nearly derailed at Bayern Munich before Arne Slot revived it**: After moving to Bayern Munich in 2022, Gravenberch found himself stuck behind Leon Goretzka, Joshua Kimmich, and Thomas Muller in the pecking order, making just 24 Bundesliga appearances in one season with only 1 goal. Many wrote him off as another young talent lost at a big club. But Arne Slot's arrival at Liverpool transformed his career -- Slot identified Gravenberch as his first-choice defensive midfielder and built the team's structure around him. The result has been one of the most dramatic career revivals in recent Premier League history.
