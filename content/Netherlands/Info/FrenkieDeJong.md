## Interesting Facts

1. **He chose Barcelona over Paris Saint-Germain and Manchester City**: When De Jong decided to leave Ajax in 2019, he was one of the most sought-after players in world football. Paris Saint-Germain and Manchester City both offered more money, but De Jong insisted on joining Barcelona because of their playing style and his admiration for the club's philosophy. He has previously stated that Johan Cruyff's legacy at Barcelona was a major factor in his decision.

2. **He once scored a goal and then immediately apologized to the opposing fans**: While playing for Ajax, De Jong scored a goal against his former club Willem II, where he had come through the youth system. After scoring, he raised his hands in a gesture of apology to the Willem II supporters, showing respect for the club that gave him his start in professional football.
