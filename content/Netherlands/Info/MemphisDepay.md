- **Free-kick specialist**: One of the best set-piece takers in international football
- **Dribbling**: Excellent one-on-one ability, combining close control with explosive acceleration
- **Physical strength**: Deceptively strong despite his relatively modest frame

## Interesting Facts

1. **He dropped his father's surname to become "Memphis"**: Born Memphis Depay, he legally dropped "Depay" (his Ghanaian father's surname) early in his career. His father abandoned the family when Memphis was four years old, and he has been estranged from him ever since. Memphis has been vocal about his difficult upbringing, raised primarily by his mother and grandparents, and has used his platform to speak about the importance of family and perseverance.

2. **He is also a recording artist and fashion designer**: Beyond football, Memphis has pursued a parallel career in music, releasing several rap and hip-hop tracks, including collaborations with notable artists. He is also known for his distinctive fashion sense, often appearing in bold outfits and accessories. His social media presence is a blend of football highlights, music promotion, and fashion statements, making him one of the most recognizable Dutch athletes off the pitch.
