## Interesting Facts

1. **He has Togolese heritage through his father**: Gakpo's father, Johnny Gakpo, is of Togolese descent and was born in Togo before moving to the Netherlands. This means Cody was technically eligible to represent Togo internationally before committing to the Netherlands. His mother is Dutch, and Cody grew up in Eindhoven, coming through PSV's academy from a young age.

2. **He completed a university degree while playing professional football**: Unlike most professional footballers who focus exclusively on their sport, Gakpo pursued higher education alongside his playing career. He studied at the Fontys University of Applied Sciences while breaking into the PSV first team, showing a commitment to personal development beyond the pitch that is rare among elite footballers.
