## Skills and Strengths

- **Aerial dominance**: Wins an extraordinary percentage of aerial duels, dominant at both ends of the pitch
- **One-on-one defending**: Rarely beaten in individual situations -- opponents actively avoid running at him
- **Composure**: Almost never looks flustered, calm under the highest pressure
- **Passing range**: Excellent at launching attacks with accurate long passes from the back
- **Leadership**: Captains both Liverpool and the Netherlands with authority and vocal organization
- **Physical presence**: At 1.93 m and 92 kg, he is an imposing figure who combines size with surprising agility
- **Recovery pace**: Despite his size, he has the speed to recover when caught high up the pitch
- **Set-piece threat**: Consistently scores goals from corners and free kicks

## Interesting Facts

1. **He was rejected by his hometown club as a teenager**: As a youth player in Breda, Van Dijk was released by the local professional club, Willem II. He was told he was too limited and not good enough. He nearly quit football entirely and considered working at a restaurant. Instead, he joined the academy at Groningen, where he developed into a professional player, eventually making his way through Celtic and Southampton before becoming the world's most expensive defender at Liverpool.

2. **He is famously unbeaten at Anfield in the Premier League for over 60 matches**: Van Dijk's home record at Anfield is one of the most remarkable statistics in modern football. He went on a run of over 60 consecutive home Premier League matches without tasting defeat, a streak that became a running joke among fans and opponents alike. When he suffered his ACL injury in October 2020, Liverpool's home form collapsed dramatically without him, underlining just how irreplaceable he is.
