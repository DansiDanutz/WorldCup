## Squad Notes

- **Captain**: Virgil van Dijk -- the commanding Liverpool centre-back leads by example
- **All-time top scorer**: Memphis Depay holds the Netherlands scoring record with 55 goals in 108 caps
- **Liverpool contingent**: Three players from Liverpool (Van Dijk, Gakpo, Gravenberch) providing built-in chemistry
- **Brighton pair**: Two players from Brighton (Verbruggen, Van Hecke) plus former Brighton man Wieffer
- **Key absence**: Xavi Simons suffered an ACL injury just 47 days before the tournament -- a devastating blow to the squad's creative options
- **Youth vs experience**: The squad ranges from 19-year-old Jorrel Hato to 34-year-olds Van Dijk and De Roon
- **Serie A connection**: Dumfries (Inter), De Roon (Atalanta), Koopmeiners (Juventus), Malen (Roma) all ply their trade in Italy
- **Tactical flexibility**: Koeman can deploy either a 4-3-3 or 3-5-2 depending on the opposition
- **Premier League dominance**: 11 of the 26 players currently play in the English Premier League
