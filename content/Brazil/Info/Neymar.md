### Skills and Strengths

Neymar is one of the most gifted playmakers and entertainers in football history. Even at 34, his technical ability and creative vision remain exceptional.

**Key strengths:**
- **Dribbling**: Perhaps the most naturally gifted dribbler of his generation; combines Brazilian flair with European efficiency
- **Vision and passing**: Sees passes nobody else sees; can unlock defenses with a single ball
- **Free kicks**: One of the best dead-ball specialists in the game
- **Creativity**: An assist machine who creates chances from nothing
- **Big-game experience**: Has performed on the biggest stages for club and country
- **Versatility**: Can play on either wing, as a No. 10, or as a second striker
- **Showmanship**: The rainbow flicks, sombreros, and elastico are part of his DNA

### Funny and Interesting Facts

**1. The Poker Face Celebration**
Neymar is famous for his elaborate goal celebrations, but the most iconic was born during the 2014 World Cup. After scoring against Cameroon, he sprinted to the corner flag and performed a "poker face" gesture -- pretending to deal cards. He later revealed it was an inside joke with his friends, who played poker together the night before. The celebration became so popular that it was added as an emote in Fortnite.

**2. The Santos Return and the Contract Clause**
When Neymar returned to Santos in 2025, his contract reportedly included a unique clause: he would receive a bonus for every rainbow flick he successfully performed in a match. While never officially confirmed, Santos staff reportedly kept a "rainbow flick counter" during games. Neymar completed at least three successful rainbow flicks in competitive matches during his return season, to the delight of the Vila Belmiro faithful.
