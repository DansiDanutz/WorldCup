## Squad Analysis

### Strengths
- **World-class attack**: Vinicius Jr. is among the best forwards on the planet; Neymar brings experience and magic
- **Goalkeeping excellence**: Alisson is widely considered a top-3 goalkeeper globally
- **Defensive leadership**: Marquinhos and Gabriel Magalhaes form a formidable center-back partnership
- **Midfield battle-hardened**: Casemiro and Bruno Guimaraes offer steel and creativity
- **Raphinha's form**: Outstanding 2025-26 season at Barcelona adds another dimension

### Weaknesses
- **Age in key positions**: Neymar (34), Casemiro (33), Alex Sandro (35) are past their physical peaks
- **Lack of a pure No. 9**: No established world-class striker in the squad
- **Left-back depth**: Alex Sandro is aging; Douglas Santos is the only cover
- **Chemistry concerns**: Ancelotti has had limited time to implement his system

### Key Tactical Setup
Ancelotti is expected to deploy a **4-3-3 or 4-2-3-1** formation:
- Alisson in goal
- Marquinhos and Gabriel as the CB pairing
- Vinicius Jr. and Raphinha on the flanks
- Neymar as the creative hub behind the main striker
- Casemiro anchoring the midfield
