### Skills and Strengths

Raphinha has developed into one of the most complete wingers in world football. His 2025-26 season at Barcelona has been the best of his career.

**Key strengths:**
- **Work rate**: Presses relentlessly; among the hardest-working wingers in elite football
- **Dribbling**: Direct, explosive, and fearless in one-on-one situations
- **Crossing**: Delivers dangerous balls into the box with both feet
- **Versatility**: Can play on either flank and adapt his game accordingly
- **Set pieces**: Excellent corner and free-kick delivery
- **Defensive contribution**: Tracks back to help his full-back, rare for an attacking player
- **Leadership**: Has emerged as a vocal leader at Barcelona

### Funny and Interesting Facts

**1. From Futsal to the Camp Nou**
Raphinha grew up playing futsal in the streets of Porto Alegre, Brazil. His first "proper" football club was a local amateur team where the players had to chip in to buy the match ball. He credits futsal for his close control and quick feet -- and still plays futsal during off-season visits to Brazil.

**2. The Leeds Legacy**
When Raphinha left Leeds United for Barcelona in 2022, he was so beloved that Leeds fans created a mural of him in the city center. He reportedly cried when saying goodbye to the Leeds squad and still watches Leeds results every weekend. He once said in an interview: "Leeds gave me my chance in big football. I will always love that club, even if people think I'm crazy."
