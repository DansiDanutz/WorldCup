## Playing Style

Šeško is a generational striking talent — a towering forward with surprising pace and exceptional technical ability for his size. Often compared to Erling Haaland for his physical profile, Šeško is more than just a target man, offering intelligent movement and link-up play.

## Key Skills
- **Physical Dominance:** Uses his size to overpower defenders
- **Pace:** Remarkably quick for a 6'5" player
- **Finishing:** Clinical with both feet and head
- **Link-Up Play:** Surprisingly good at holding the ball and playing in teammates
- **Aerial Threat:** Dominant in the air at both ends
