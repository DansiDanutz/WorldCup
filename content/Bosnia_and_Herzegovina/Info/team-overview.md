# Bosnia and Herzegovina - FIFA World Cup 2026

## Group A: Mexico, South Africa, South Korea, Bosnia and Herzegovina

## Team History

### The Dragons

Bosnia and Herzegovina qualified for their second World Cup, returning to the global stage after their debut in 2014. The nation of 3.5 million people has produced world-class talent despite its relatively small population.

### World Cup Record

| Year | Host | Result | Key Moment |
|------|------|--------|------------|
| 2014 | Brazil | Group Stage | Debut World Cup; scored in every match |

### The Dzeko Generation

Edin Dzeko has been the face of Bosnian football for over a decade. Now in his final tournament at 40, he leads a new generation hoping to surpass the achievements of 2014.

---

## Playing Style

Bosnia plays a **technical 4-2-3-1** emphasizing:
- **Dzeko as focal point**: Everything flows through their legendary striker
- **Technical midfielders**: Players comfortable on the ball
- **Set pieces**: Dzeko and tall defenders are aerial threats
- **Counter-attacking**: Quick transitions through technical players
- **Experience**: Older squad with significant tournament know-how

---

## World Cup 2026 Prospects

Drawn with Mexico, South Africa, and South Korea, Bosnia has a realistic chance of advancing to the knockout stage.

**Key Question**: Can Dzeko's farewell tournament end in glory?
