### Skills and Strengths

Edin Dzeko is one of the most underrated strikers of his generation, a complete forward who has scored prolifically for over two decades.

**Key strengths:**
- **Finishing**: Elite goalscorer with both feet and head
- **Aerial ability**: One of the best headers in football history
- **Hold-up play**: Brings teammates into play superbly
- **Movement**: Intelligent runs that find space
- **Longevity**: Still scoring at 40
- **Experience**: 135+ caps; Bosnia's all-time leading scorer
