## Squad Analysis

### Strengths
- **Edin Dzeko**: Legendary striker; still scores at the highest level
- **Miralem Pjanic**: World-class playmaker despite his age
- **Sead Kolasinac**: Experienced defender at top Italian club
- **Technical quality**: Squad is comfortable on the ball
- **Tournament experience**: Many players have been to major tournaments

### Weaknesses
- **Aging squad**: Dzeko (40), Pjanic (36), Kolasinac (32) are past their peaks
- **Lack of young talent**: Few emerging stars to replace legends
- **Defensive pace**: Can be exposed by fast attackers
- **Squad depth**: Thin behind the starters
