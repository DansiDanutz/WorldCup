# Bosnia and Herzegovina vs Qatar — Group B, Matchday 2

**June 17, 2026** | BMO Field, Toronto | FIFA World Cup 2026

---

## Head-to-Head History

**No previous meetings.** First-ever encounter between these nations.

---

## Key Player Matchups

### Benjamin Sesko vs Boualem Khoukhi
Sesko's pace and power against Khoukhi's versatility and 80+ caps of experience. The Bosnian wonderkid against Qatar's defensive Swiss army knife.

### Miralem Pjanic vs Karim Boudiaf
Two veterans orchestrating from midfield. Pjanic's Juventus/Barcelona pedigree against Boudiaf's 80+ caps and leadership. The midfield battle will be chess between grandmasters.

### Edin Dzeko vs Bassam Al-Rawi
The veteran Fenerbahce striker against Al-Duhail's centre-back. Dzeko's IQ and movement against Al-Rawi's positioning. Experience vs experience.

---

## Storyline: Two Small Nations, Big Dreams

Bosnia (population 3.5 million) and Qatar (population 3 million) prove that World Cup football isn't just for the giants. Both nations have invested heavily in their football infrastructure — Bosnia through diaspora talent in European academies, Qatar through the Aspire Academy's scientific approach. Barbarez's Dragons play with Balkan fire; Márquez's Al-Annabi play with Iberian finesse. The contrast in styles makes this appointment viewing.

---

## Tactical Preview

**Bosnia (4-2-3-1)** — Physical, direct, clinical on the counter. Sesko is the outlet, Pjanic the creator, Kolasinac the enforcer.

**Qatar (4-3-3)** — Possession-dominant, technically clean, patient. Almoez Ali provides the finishing touch, Akram Afif the creativity.

---

## X-Factor: Akram Afif's Creativity

Qatar's most gifted player has 80+ caps and the vision to unlock any defense. If Bosnia sits deep, Afif's passing range and dribbling can find the gaps. His 2019 Asian Cup performance (MVP) proved he can dominate on the big stage.
