## Interesting Facts and Stories

### 1. The Goal Against Cameroon That Changed Everything

At the 2022 World Cup in Qatar, Embolo scored the winning goal in Switzerland's opening match against Cameroon -- the country of his birth. Born in Yaounde, Cameroon, Embolo moved to Switzerland as a child and chose to represent his adopted country. After scoring, he refused to celebrate, standing still with his arms raised in a gesture of respect for his birth nation. The image went viral and was widely praised as one of the most powerful moments in World Cup history. Embolo later revealed that his mother called him in tears after the match, proud but also conflicted about the goal. It remains the defining moment of his international career.

### 2. From Refugee Camp to World Cup Star

Embolo's life story is one of football's most remarkable journeys. He was born in Yaounde, Cameroon, and his parents separated when he was young. His mother eventually moved to Switzerland, and Breel followed, growing up in Basel. He has spoken about how football saved him from a difficult childhood, giving him purpose and direction. He was discovered by FC Basel's youth system and rose rapidly through the ranks, making his professional debut at 17. Despite the wealth and fame that came with his success, Embolo has remained grounded, frequently visiting Cameroon and supporting charitable causes in both his birth and adopted countries. He holds both Cameroonian and Swiss citizenship and speaks French, German, and English fluently.
