## Skills and Strengths

- **Ball-Playing Ability:** One of the best ball-playing defenders in world football; comfortable driving forward with the ball or playing line-breaking passes
- **Composure:** Rarely flustered, even under intense pressing; his calmness spreads through the back line
- **Tackling:** Excellent timing in challenges; rarely commits reckless fouls
- **Aerial Ability:** Strong in the air both defensively and offensively from set pieces
- **Tactical Intelligence:** Reads the game at an elite level, positioning himself to intercept and cut out danger
- **Versatility:** Can play in a back three or back four, and has even been used in defensive midfield
- **Technical Skills:** Famously demonstrated his ability to juggle and perform tricks with the ball, showcasing exceptional technical ability for a defender

## Interesting Facts and Stories

### 1. The Human Calculator

Akanji is famously one of the most intellectually gifted footballers in the world. He speaks five languages fluently (German, English, French, Portuguese, and Spanish), is an accomplished chess player, and has been known to solve complex mathematical puzzles in his spare time. During his time at Manchester City, teammates nicknamed him "The Professor" because of his encyclopedic knowledge on virtually any subject. In interviews, he has discussed his interest in psychology and philosophy, and he approaches football with an analytical mindset that has made him one of the most tactically astute defenders in the game.

### 2. From Late Bloomer to Treble Winner

Akanji's path to the top was not straightforward. He was not part of any major youth academy and played for FC Winterthur in the Swiss lower divisions before FC Basel signed him at age 20. Many Swiss observers considered him a late developer. Even at Borussia Dortmund, he had periods where he struggled for form and was criticized by fans. It was his move to Manchester City in September 2022 for a surprisingly modest fee (reported at around 17 million euros) that transformed his career. Pep Guardiola immediately trusted him, and Akanji became a cornerstone of the greatest single season in English football history -- the 2022-23 treble. His rise from Swiss second-tier football to Champions League winner in less than eight years is one of football's most remarkable trajectories.
