### The AFCON 2024 Miracle
Fae's rise to the top is one of football's most extraordinary stories:

1. **January 2024:** Ivory Coast, hosting AFCON, suffered an embarrassing 4-0 defeat to Equatorial Guinea in the group stage. Coach Jean-Louis Gasset was sacked.
2. **Emerse Fae, the assistant coach,** was promoted to interim manager with the team on the brink of elimination.
3. Against all odds, Fae led the team through the knockout rounds with a series of dramatic victories:
   - Round of 16: Defeated defending champions Senegal on penalties
   - Quarter-finals: Beat Mali in extra time
   - Semi-finals: Defeated DR Congo 1-0
   - **Final: Beat Nigeria 2-1 to win the Africa Cup of Nations on home soil**
4. It was the first time a host nation had won AFCON since 2006 (Egypt) and the first time an interim manager had won the tournament.
5. Fae was immediately appointed as permanent head coach.

## Tactical Approach

Fae's style is built on defensive discipline combined with explosive attacking transitions:

### Defensive Solidity
- The qualifying campaign (zero goals conceded in 10 matches) reflects Fae's obsession with defensive organization.
- He demands a compact shape, with midfielders dropping deep to create a low block when out of possession.
- The center-back partnership of Diomande and Ndicka is the foundation of everything.

### Controlled Counter-Attacking
- Ivory Coast do not dominate possession for its own sake. Instead, they absorb pressure and strike on the break.
- The pace of Diallo, Adingra, and Yan Diomande makes the team lethal in transition.
- Seko Fofana's driving runs from midfield add an extra dimension to the counter-attack.

### Flexibility
- Unlike many African coaches, Fae is tactically flexible. He adjusts his system based on the opponent.
- Against weaker teams, Ivory Coast can play a high line and press aggressively.
- Against stronger teams, they drop deep and use the pace of their forwards on the counter.

### Man-Management
- Fae is known for his calm demeanor and ability to connect with players. As a former international himself, he commands immediate respect.
- He successfully integrated several French-born dual-nationals into the squad, creating a unified team culture.
- His handling of the AFCON crisis — taking over a demoralized squad and turning them into champions — is testament to his man-management skills.
