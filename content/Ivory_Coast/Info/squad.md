## Key Tactical Notes

- Fae operates with a **4-2-3-1** or **4-3-3** formation.
- **Franck Kessie** is the midfield anchor and emotional leader; his partnership with Ibrahim Sangare or Seko Fofana provides the platform for the attack.
- **Ousmane Diomande** and **Evan Ndicka** form one of the most formidable center-back partnerships in the tournament.
- The forward line is devastating: Amad Diallo's dribbling, Simon Adingra's pace, and Yan Diomande's finishing give Ivory Coast multiple goal threats.
- **Nicolas Pepe** provides experienced depth from the bench — his 45 caps and 10 goals make him a valuable impact substitute.
- The squad's strength in depth is remarkable — even the backup forwards (Bonny, Diakite, Guessand) would start for most African nations.
