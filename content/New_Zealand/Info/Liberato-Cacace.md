## Profile
- **Full Name:** Liberato Salvatore Cacace
- **Nickname:** "Libby"
- **Position:** Left-Back / Wing-Back
- **Date of Birth:** September 27, 2000
- **Place of Birth:** Wellington, New Zealand

## Playing Style

Cacace is an attack-minded left-back who combines Italian defensive schooling with natural Kiwi tenacity. Comfortable in possession and aggressive in the tackle, he provides width and attacking impetus from the left flank. His Serie A experience has refined his tactical awareness considerably.

## Key Skills
- **Crossing:** Excellent delivery from the left flank with his preferred left foot
- **Defensive Positioning:** Well-drilled in Italian defensive methodology
- **Stamina:** Capable of repeated sprints up and down the left touchline
- **Technical Ability:** Comfortable playing out from the back under pressure
- **Tactical Flexibility:** Can play as a traditional full-back or an attacking wing-back
