# Sarpreet Singh

## Profile
- **Full Name:** Sarpreet Singh
- **Nickname:** "Sarp"
- **Position:** Attacking Midfielder / Winger
- **Date of Birth:** February 20, 1999
- **Place of Birth:** Auckland, New Zealand
- **Height:** 1.75 m (5 ft 9 in)
- **Current Club:** Hansa Rostock (2. Bundesliga)
- **Nationality:** New Zealander / Indian

## Playing Style

Singh is a technically gifted playmaker with exceptional close control and creativity. Trained in Bayern Munich's academy system, he possesses a European tactical education that sets him apart. He drifts between the lines, finding pockets of space to receive and distribute the ball.

## Key Skills
- **Ball Control:** Exceptional first touch and close dribbling in tight areas
- **Vision:** Sees passes and angles that unlock defenses
- **Set Pieces:** Accurate free-kick and corner delivery
- **Passing Range:** Short combination play and longer switches of play
- **Composure:** Rarely rushed in possession, plays at his own tempo
