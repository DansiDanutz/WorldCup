# Marko Stamenic

## Profile
- **Full Name:** Marko Stamenic
- **Nickname:** "Marko"
- **Position:** Central Midfielder
- **Date of Birth:** December 19, 2002
- **Place of Birth:** Wellington, New Zealand
- **Height:** 1.85 m (6 ft 1 in)

## Playing Style

Stamenic is a modern, all-action central midfielder who combines physicality with technical ability. Despite his young age, he plays with remarkable maturity, reading the game beyond his years. He box-to-box capability, contributing defensively while also joining attacks with well-timed runs.

## Key Skills
- **Passing:** Crisp, accurate distribution over all distances
- **Physicality:** Strong in challenges and effective at shielding the defense
- **Ball Carrying:** Can drive forward with the ball from deep positions
- **Tactical Intelligence:** Understands positioning and pressing triggers
- **Versatility:** Comfortable as a #6, #8, or even as a #10
