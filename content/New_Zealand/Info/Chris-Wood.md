# Chris Wood

## Profile
- **Full Name:** Christopher David Wood
- **Nickname:** "Woody"
- **Position:** Striker
- **Date of Birth:** December 7, 1991
- **Place of Birth:** Auckland, New Zealand
- **Height:** 1.91 m (6 ft 3 in)
- **Current Club:** Nottingham Forest (Premier League)
- **Nationality:** New Zealander
- **Captain:** Yes

## Playing Style

Chris Wood is a classic target man and one of the Premier League's most underrated strikers. He combines physical dominance with surprisingly nimble feet and intelligent movement. As New Zealand's captain and all-time leading scorer, he is the heartbeat of the All Whites — the player opponents fear most.

## Key Skills
- **Aerial Dominance:** Wins the majority of headers in both boxes, lethal from crosses
- **Hold-Up Play:** Shields the ball brilliantly, bringing teammates into play
- **Finishing:** Clinical with both feet and head inside the penalty area
- **Penalty Box Instinct:** Always seems to be in the right place at the right time
- **Leadership:** Inspirational captain who leads by example with tireless effort
