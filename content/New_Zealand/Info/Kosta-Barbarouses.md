# Kosta Barbarouses

## Profile
- **Full Name:** Konstantinos "Kosta" Barbarouses
- **Nickname:** "Kosta"
- **Position:** Winger / Forward
- **Date of Birth:** February 19, 1990
- **Place of Birth:** Wellington, New Zealand
- **Height:** 1.73 m (5 ft 8 in)
- **Current Club:** Wellington Phoenix (A-League)
- **Nationality:** New Zealander / Greek

## Playing Style

Barbarouses is a speedy, experienced winger who has been a mainstay of New Zealand football for over 15 years. His electric pace and dribbling ability make him a constant threat on the counter-attack. Though no longer the starter he once was, his experience and impact from the bench are invaluable.

## Key Skills
- **Pace:** Still possesses explosive acceleration despite his age
- **Dribbling:** Quick feet and tricks to beat defenders in wide areas
- **Experience:** Over 300 professional appearances across multiple leagues
- **Versatility:** Can play on either wing or as a second striker
- **Mentorship:** A guiding voice for younger players in the squad

## Fun Facts
- Born to Greek parents who emigrated to New Zealand — named after his grandfather
- Was in the 2010 World Cup squad as a 20-year-old, making him one of the youngest players at the tournament
- His 16-year gap between World Cup appearances (2010-2026) is one of the longest in football history
- Has won three A-League championships with two different clubs
- Runs a football academy for underprivileged youth in Wellington
