## Thumbnail Concepts

### Layout A: "Player Face + Big Number"
- Left: Pixar-style player character with intense expression
- Right: Huge text "[TEAM] ×3 = $$$"
- Background: Team colors gradient + subtle stadium
- Accent: Golden trophy icon top-right

### Layout B: "Before/After Shock"
- Split screen
- Left: Sad fan character (team lost last tournament)
- Right: Same fan exploding with joy (team winning now)
- Middle: "2022 → 2026" with arrow
- Text: "THE CURSE IS BROKEN?"

### Layout C: "Mystery Supporter"
- Shadowy figure in team colors, back to camera
- Fog/stadium lights atmosphere
- Text: "The Fan Nobody Knows"
- Small reveal: glowing eyes or silhouette hint

### Layout D: "Pick 3 Math"
- Three player characters in a row
- Math symbols between them: + × ÷
- Big result number at bottom
- Text: "My 3 Teams = 47 Points"
