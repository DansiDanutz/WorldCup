## The real blocker (honest diagnosis)

| Video | Views | Why |
|---|---|---|
| Launch Film | 2,000 | **Paid 72h campaign pushed it.** Distribution, not content. |
| Ep1 MEX–RSA | 627 | **Premiere + day-before-match timing** + campaign tail. |
| Ep2 KOR–CZE | 83 | Plain upload, after the match window, zero promotion. |
| Ep3 CAN–BIH | 11 | Same — and it's 9h old with no push at all. |

**A new channel gets ~zero impressions from YouTube on its own.** The algorithm
only starts testing long-form videos when it sees signals from: (1) Shorts
traffic, (2) external clicks (site/WhatsApp/Telegram/X), (3) premiere
notifications, (4) strong CTR titles/thumbnails when impressions do come.
The Launch Film proved the machine works when fed. Eps 2–3 got zero of all
four. **Content quality was never the blocker — fuel was.**
