# WorldCup26 — YouTube Channel Strategy

## Channel Identity

**Channel Name:** WorldCup26 Legends
**Tagline:** "Pick 3 Teams. Change Everything."
**Niche:** FIFA World Cup 2026 Prediction Game + Football Storytelling
**Target Audience:** Football fans aged 18-45, fantasy sports players, casual gamers

## Visual Branding

- **Logo:** Pixar-style golden trophy with "26" glowing inside
- **Color Palette:** World Cup gold (#FFD700), pitch green (#2E8B57), sky blue (#87CEEB)
- **Font:** Bold sans-serif for titles, playful rounded font for numbers
- **Avatar:** Animated mascot "Cuppie" — a golden World Cup trophy with arms, legs, and expressive eyes

## Content Pillars

| Pillar | Frequency | Content Type |
|--------|-----------|--------------|
| **Team Spotlights** | 3x/week | Deep dives into each of the 48 teams |
| **Pick 3 Strategy** | 2x/week | Tips on team combinations, coefficients, dark horses |
| **Match Reactions** | Daily during WC | Live-style reactions to goals, upsets, drama |
| **Supporter Stories** | 1x/week | Fan culture, ultras, mystery legends from each nation |
| **Behind the Scenes** | 1x/week | How the game works, dev updates, community highlights |

## Upload Schedule

- **Monday:** Team Spotlight (major nation)
- **Tuesday:** Pick 3 Strategy
- **Wednesday:** Team Spotlight (underdog)
- **Thursday:** Supporter Stories / Mystery Legends
- **Friday:** Pick 3 Strategy + Weekend Preview
- **Saturday:** Match Reaction (during tournament)
- **Sunday:** Weekly Recap + Top Picks

## Mystery / Supporter Hook

Every video includes a "Mystery Supporter" segment — a legendary, almost mythical fan figure from that nation's football history. Think:
- Argentina: "El Abuelo de la Bombonera" — a ghostly old man seen at every Boca/Argentina game since 1940
- Brazil: "The Feathered Prophet" — a shaman from the Amazon who predicted all 5 Brazilian World Cup wins
- England: "The Cursed Gentlemen" — a 1966 attendee who hasn't missed a game but brings bad luck

These become collectible characters in the game and recurring Easter eggs in videos.
