## Coaching Career

Koubek is one of Czech football's most experienced coaches, having managed virtually every major Czech club:

- **Viktoria Plzen** — Multiple league titles
- **Slavia Prague** — Domestic success
- **Sparta Prague** — Historic rivalry
- **Czech Republic National Team** (2024–present) — Appointed at age 73

### Philosophy
A traditionalist who believes in disciplined defending and efficient attacking. At 74, he is the oldest coach at the 2026 World Cup.
