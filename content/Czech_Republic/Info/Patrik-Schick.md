## Career Journey

- **Sparta Prague** (2014–2016) — Czech league breakthrough
- **Sampdoria** (2016–2019) — Serie A experience
- **Roma** (loan 2017–2018) — Scored memorable goals in Serie A
- **RB Leipzig** (loan 2019–2020) — Bundesliga adaptation
- **Bayer Leverkusen** (2020–present) — Bundesliga champion 2023/24, Xabi Alonso's key striker

### Euro 2024 Hero
Schick scored a stunning panenka penalty in Euro 2024, showcasing his trademark confidence and composure. He was one of the tournament's standout strikers.

---

## Playing Style

- **Clinical finishing**: One of Europe's most reliable finishers
- **Aerial threat**: 6'3", dominant in the air
- **Hold-up play**: Strong back-to-goal ability
- **Composure**: Ice-cold in front of goal, especially in big moments
- **Panenka specialist**: Known for his audacious chipped penalties

---

## Fun Facts

- Scored a goal from the halfway line at Euro 2020 vs Scotland (45.4 yards)
- Was teammates with Patrik Berger's son at youth level — continuing the Czech football dynasty
- Won the Bundesliga with Leverkusen's first-ever unbeaten league season (2023/24)
- His panenka at Euro 2024 went viral worldwide
