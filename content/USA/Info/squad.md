## Squad Analysis

### Strengths
- **Pulisic**: World-class winger in top form at AC Milan
- **Midfield athleticism**: Adams, McKennie, and Musah form a dynamic trio
- **Youthful energy**: Most starters are under 27
- **Home advantage**: Playing every match in front of American crowds
- **Pochettino's pressing**: Fits the squad's athletic profile perfectly

### Weaknesses
- **Goalkeeper**: Turner is solid but not elite
- **Center-back depth**: Thin behind Richards and Ream
- **Striker inconsistency**: Balogun and Sargent have struggled for goals
- **Pressure of hosting**: First-time host nation expectations
