### Skills and Strengths

Weston McKennie is a box-to-box powerhouse who brings energy, goals, and tactical intelligence to the USA midfield.

**Key strengths:**
- **Aerial ability**: Among the best heading midfielders in the world
- **Stamina**: Covers enormous ground every match
- **Goalscoring**: Arrives late in the box to score crucial goals
- **Versatility**: Can play as a 6, 8, or even wing-back
- **Physicality**: Wins duels and imposes himself on opponents
- **Tactical IQ**: Reads the game well under Allegri at Juventus
