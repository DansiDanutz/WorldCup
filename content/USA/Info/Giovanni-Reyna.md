### Skills and Strengths

Gio Reyna is the USA's most creative player, a technically gifted attacker who can unlock defenses with dribbling, passing, and shooting.

**Key strengths:**
- **Dribbling**: Exceptional close control in tight spaces
- **Vision**: Creates chances with defense-splitting passes
- **Finishing**: Scores from distance and inside the box
- **Versatility**: Plays across the front line and midfield
- **Pedigree**: Son of US captain Claudio Reyna; grew up at Dortmund's academy
- **Big potential**: Still developing into a world-class talent
