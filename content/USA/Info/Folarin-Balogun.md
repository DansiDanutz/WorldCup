### Skills and Strengths

Folarin Balogun is the USA's most natural goalscorer, a predatory striker who switched allegiance from England to represent the USMNT.

**Key strengths:**
- **Finishing**: Lethal inside the box; scores with both feet
- **Movement**: Intelligent runs to find space between defenders
- **Pace**: Quick over short distances to get in behind
- **Youth**: Still developing into a complete striker
- **Competitive drive**: Chose USA to be the main man
- **Link-up play**: Improving ability to combine with wingers
