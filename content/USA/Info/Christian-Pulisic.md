### Skills and Strengths

Christian Pulisic is the face of American soccer, a technically gifted winger who thrives in one-on-one situations.

**Key strengths:**
- **Dribbling**: Elite close control and acceleration
- **Finishing**: Improved significantly at AC Milan; now a reliable goalscorer
- **Big-game impact**: Scores crucial goals in high-pressure moments
- **Work rate**: Tracks back and defends diligently
- **Experience**: Played at Chelsea and Dortmund before Milan
- **Leadership**: Captain who leads by example
