## Key Player Matchups

### Christian Pulisic vs Gustavo Gomez
The USA captain against Paraguay's defensive rock. Pulisic's dribbling and direct running will test Gomez's positioning and physicality. The AC Milan winger thrives in one-v-one situations, but Gomez — with 88+ caps and years of experience at Palmeiras — is one of South America's most battle-hardened defenders. This is the battle that could define the match.

### Folarin Balogun vs Omar Alderete
Balogun's intelligent movement against Alderete's aggressive defending. The Monaco striker's predatory instincts inside the box will face a Sunderland defender who thrives in physical duels. If Balogun can find space between Gomez and Alderete, the USA has a clear path to goal.

### Tyler Adams vs Julio Enciso
The midfield destroyer vs "La Joya." Adams' relentless pressing could suffocate Enciso's creative space, but if the Paraguayan playmaker finds room to operate, he possesses the talent to unlock any defense. Enciso's Goal of the Season against Manchester City proved he can produce magic against elite opponents.

## Storyline: The Host's Burden

The weight of a nation rests on Pulisic's shoulders. In 1994, the USA hosted its first World Cup and ignited a football revolution. Now, 32 years later, the tournament returns to American soil with a team that many believe is the most talented in USMNT history.

But Paraguay carries its own emotional burden — a 16-year World Cup absence that haunted a nation of just 7 million people squeezed between Brazil and Argentina. Coach Gustavo Alfaro built a team that survived CONMEBOL qualifying, the most brutal pathway in world football. They earned their spot by enduring what no other team in this group had to face.

For Paraguay, this is more than a football match. It's a declaration that they belong back on the world stage. For the USA, anything less than victory in the opener would send shockwaves of doubt through a team carrying the expectations of 330 million people.

---

## Tactical Preview

**USA (4-3-3):** Expect aggressive high pressing from the opening whistle. Pulisic and Reyna will look to stretch Paraguay's compact defensive shape, with McKennie arriving late from midfield. Set pieces are a major weapon — the Americans' physical advantage could prove decisive.

**Paraguay (4-4-2 / 4-2-3-1):** Alfaro will set up to absorb pressure and strike on the counter. Almiron's pace and Enciso's creativity on the break are genuine threats. Paraguay will be disciplined, physical, and happy to concede possession. Their CONMEBOL qualifying campaign taught them to be comfortable without the ball.

---

## X-Factor

**Home crowd energy.** AT&T Stadium will be packed with American fans creating an atmosphere Paraguay has rarely experienced. But the pressure cuts both ways — if Paraguay scores first, the silence of 80,000 expectant fans could become the USA's biggest enemy.
