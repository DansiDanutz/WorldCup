# Edgar Bárcenas

## Profile
- **Full Name:** Edgar Yoel Bárcenas Herrera
- **Nickname:** "El Mago" (The Magician)
- **Position:** Attacking Midfielder / Winger
- **Date of Birth:** October 23, 1995
- **Place of Birth:** Colón, Panama
- **Height:** 1.72 m (5 ft 8 in)
- **Current Club:** Club Tijuana (Liga MX)
- **Nationality:** Panamanian

## Playing Style

Bárcenas is a creative playmaker with exceptional dribbling ability and flair. His low center of gravity and quick feet make him a nightmare for defenders in one-on-one situations. He operates primarily on the left wing but drifts inside to influence play centrally, acting as Panama's primary creative force.

## Key Skills
- **Dribbling:** Elusive close control and trickery to beat multiple defenders
- **Creativity:** Sees and executes passes others don't envision
- **Free Kicks:** Dangerous set-piece specialist with curl and dip
- **Acceleration:** Quick bursts of pace to escape tight marking
- **Big-Game Temperament:** Raises his performance in high-stakes matches
