## Playing Style

Murillo is an attacking right-back known for his explosive pace, tireless running, and ability to contribute in the final third. He combines defensive solidity with a genuine threat going forward, making him one of Panama's most important players. His experience in European football has elevated his tactical understanding.

## Key Skills
- **Pace and Stamina:** Can sprint the length of the pitch repeatedly
- **Crossing:** Whipped deliveries from the right flank create scoring opportunities
- **Defensive 1v1:** Strong in individual defensive battles
- **Overlap Runs:** Times his forward runs perfectly to create width
- **Versatility:** Can play as a wing-back in a back five or as a traditional full-back
