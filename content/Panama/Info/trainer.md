## Playing Career

- Played as a striker for **Barcelona**, **Villarreal**, and **Sporting Gijon** in Spain
- Born in Denmark but raised in Spain — dual nationality
- Played for Denmark's youth national teams
- Career spanned the 1990s and early 2000s

---

## Coaching Career

### Key Roles
- **APOEL** (Cyprus) — Won the Cypriot league and led the team to the Champions League group stage
- **Leeds United** (England) — Brief stint in the Championship
- **Union SG** (Belgium) — Built a strong team before departing for Panama
- **Panama National Team** (2020-present) — Qualified for the 2026 World Cup through CONCACAF

---

## Tactical Approach

**Formation:** 5-4-1 / 4-5-1

- **Defensive discipline**: Organized and compact; difficult to break down
- **Counter-attacking**: Uses pace on the wings to punish overcommitted opponents
- **Set pieces**: Major focus — Panama trains set pieces extensively
- **Team spirit**: Emphasizes unity and fighting spirit over individual talent

### Philosophy
"We know we're not the biggest team, but we have the biggest heart. We will fight for every ball."
