# Aníbal Godoy

## Profile
- **Full Name:** Aníbal Bernardo Godoy Hernández
- **Nickname:** "El Burro" (The Worker)
- **Position:** Defensive Midfielder
- **Date of Birth:** February 2, 1990
- **Place of Birth:** Panama City, Panama
- **Height:** 1.76 m (5 ft 9 in)
- **Current Club:** Nashville SC (MLS)
- **Nationality:** Panamanian

## Playing Style

Godoy is a tough-tackling defensive midfielder who serves as Panama's midfield anchor. Known for his positional discipline, intelligent reading of the game, and ability to break up opposition attacks. He provides the defensive shield that allows Panama's more creative players to flourish.

## Key Skills
- **Tackling:** Exceptional timing in challenges, rarely commits fouls
- **Positioning:** Reads the game to intercept passes and cut off passing lanes
- **Leadership:** Vocal organizer in midfield, communicates constantly with teammates
- **Aerial Ability:** Surprisingly effective in the air despite his height
- **Passing:** Simple, efficient distribution that maintains team shape
