## Playing Style

Carrasquilla is a technically gifted central midfielder known for his exceptional ball control, vision, and passing range. He operates as a deep-lying playmaker who dictates the tempo of Panama's play. His low center of gravity allows him to navigate tight spaces and retain possession under pressure.

## Key Skills
- **Passing Range:** Capable of executing short combination plays and long diagonal switches with equal precision
- **Ball Retention:** Outstanding close control in congested midfield areas
- **Work Rate:** Covers significant ground, contributing both defensively and in attack
- **Set Piece Delivery:** Reliable from corners and free kicks
- **Composure:** Rarely flustered under opposition pressing
