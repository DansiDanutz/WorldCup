**Date:** June 22, 2026 | **Tournament:** FIFA World Cup 2026

## Head-to-Head History

---

## The Storyline: The Underdog's Last Stand

Panama's World Cup campaign likely comes down to this final group match. Against England and Ghana, they were underdogs. Against Croatia — a nation of 3.9 million that has reached a World Cup final — they remain underdogs. But Panama has spent their entire footballing history defying odds.

The Canaleros' strength lies in their togetherness. Thomas Christiansen has built a team that fights for each other, covers every blade of grass, and refuses to accept defeat until the final whistle. Against Croatia's technical excellence, Panama will rely on heart, discipline, and the occasional moment of inspiration.

For Croatia, this match is about professional execution. If they need a result for advancement, their tournament experience should prevail. If they've already qualified, rotation could give Panama an opening.

**Croatia (4-3-3):** Patient possession, midfield control, and patient build-up. Croatia will dominate the ball and try to unlock Panama's defense through Modric's passing range and Kramaric's movement.
