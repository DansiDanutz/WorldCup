## Career History

### St Mirren (2012-2015)
- Came through the youth system at St Mirren
- Made his debut as a 17-year-old
- Won the **Scottish League Cup** in 2013, scoring in the semi-final
- Made 96 appearances and established himself as one of Scotland's brightest young talents

### Hibernian (2015-2018)
- Signed for Hibernian, initially on loan before making it permanent
- Was instrumental in Hibernian's promotion back to the Scottish Premiership
- Won the **Scottish Cup** in 2016 -- Hibs' first since 1902, ending the "Leith Curse"
- Scored a famous goal in the final against Rangers
- His performances attracted interest from Celtic, Rangers, and English clubs
- Made 111 appearances, scoring 15 goals

### Aston Villa (2018 -- Present)
- Signed for Aston Villa in August 2018 for around GBP 2.5 million -- widely regarded as one of the bargains of the decade
- Was instrumental in Villa's promotion to the Premier League via the 2019 playoff final
- Scored in the playoff semi-final against West Brom
- Established himself as a Premier League standout and Aston Villa captain
- Under Unai Emery, McGinn's game reached another level
- Led Aston Villa to Champions League qualification for the 2024-25 season
- Won **Aston Villa Player of the Year** multiple times
- Made 250+ appearances for the club

## Playing Style and Strengths

### All-Action Midfielder
- McGinn is the definition of a "box-to-box" midfielder
- Covers enormous distances every match, contributing at both ends of the pitch
- Combines physical aggression with surprising technical quality
- Thrives in chaotic, high-tempo matches where his energy becomes decisive

### Goal Threat
- 20 goals from 86 Scotland caps is an excellent return for a midfielder
- Has a knack for arriving late in the box and finishing calmly
- Scores a variety of goals -- headers, long-range strikes, close-range finishes
- His goal in the 2016 Scottish Cup final is among the most celebrated in Hibernian history

### Leadership
- Named Aston Villa captain, a testament to his character and influence
- Vice-captain of Scotland behind Andy Robertson
- Vocal presence on the pitch, constantly organizing and motivating
- Emery trusted McGinn to be the emotional heartbeat of the Villa team

### Technical Ability
- Often underestimated technically due to his physical playing style
- Excellent at receiving the ball under pressure and turning away from markers
- His passing range has improved significantly under Emery's coaching
- Set-piece delivery from wide areas is a weapon for both club and country

## Interesting Facts

1. **The Bargain of the Decade** -- Aston Villa signed McGinn for just GBP 2.5 million from Hibernian in 2018. Within two years, he was valued at over GBP 40 million and had helped the club achieve promotion to the Premier League. Multiple reports suggested Arsenal, Manchester United, and Tottenham all attempted to sign him, but McGinn remained loyal to Villa. The transfer fee is routinely cited in Scottish football as one of the worst deals Hibernian ever made.

2. **The Famous "McGinn Hop"** -- McGinn is known for a distinctive pre-match ritual where he hops over the touchline onto the pitch before every game. The superstition dates back to his St Mirren days, when he hopped onto the pitch before his debut and played well. He has done it before every match since -- for club and country, over 400 professional games and counting. When a teammate accidentally stepped on the pitch before him before a Villa match, McGinn reportedly made him come back off so he could hop on first.
