## Squad Analysis

### Strengths
- **Experienced spine:** Robertson (94 caps), McGinn (86 caps), Hanley (68 caps), Gordon (84 caps) provide battle-tested leadership
- **Left side dominance:** Robertson and Tierney give Scotland arguably one of the best left-flank partnerships in international football
- **Goal threat from midfield:** McTominay (15 goals) and McGinn (20 goals) contribute significantly from midfield positions
- **Tournament experience:** Several players have featured in the 2020 European Championship and 2024 Euros

### Weaknesses
- **Lack of elite strikers:** No established goalscorer at the highest club level
- **Right-back depth:** Behind Nathan Patterson, the options are limited
- **Creative midfield:** Billy Gilmour's pre-tournament injury left a void in the creative department
- **Age profile:** Several key players are over 30, which may affect stamina in tournament conditions

### Key Absence
- **Billy Gilmour** -- Ruled out on May 30 due to injury; replaced by 19-year-old Tyler Fletcher (son of former Scotland international Darren Fletcher)
