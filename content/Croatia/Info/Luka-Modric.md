# Luka Modrić

## Profile
- **Full Name:** Luka Modrić
- **Nickname:** "The Maestro"
- **Position:** Central Midfielder
- **Date of Birth:** September 9, 1985
- **Place of Birth:** Zadar, Croatia
- **Height:** 1.72 m (5 ft 8 in)
- **Current Club:** Real Madrid (La Liga)
- **Nationality:** Croatian

## Playing Style

Modrić is one of the greatest midfielders in football history. His vision, passing range, and ball control remain world-class even in his late 30s. He dictates the tempo of every match he plays, orchestrating attacks with surgical precision while also contributing defensively.

## Key Skills
- **Passing Range:** Can execute any pass — short, long, chipped, driven — with perfect weight
- **Vision:** Sees plays develop before anyone else on the pitch
- **Ball Control:** Immaculate first touch under any pressure
- **Work Rate:** Covers immense ground despite his age
- **Leadership:** Croatia's inspirational captain, leads by example

- The oldest outfield player in Real Madrid history
- Has won more Champions League titles (6) than any player not named Paco Gento
- His signature outside-of-the-boot pass is taught in football academies worldwide
