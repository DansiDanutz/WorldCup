- **Date of Birth:** May 6, 1994

## Playing Style

Kovačić is a supremely gifted ball-carrying midfielder who combines elegance with effectiveness. His ability to glide past opponents with the ball at his feet is among the best in world football. At Manchester City, he has added tactical discipline to his natural talent.

## Key Skills
- **Ball Carrying:** Exceptional at driving forward from midfield with close control
- **Press Resistance:** Calm under pressure, rarely loses possession
- **Technical Ability:** Perfect first touch and clean passing technique
- **Versatility:** Can play as a #6, #8, or advanced playmaker
- **Big Game Experience:** Won Champions League with Chelsea and multiple trophies with City
