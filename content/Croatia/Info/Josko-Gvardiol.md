# Joško Gvardiol

## Profile
- **Full Name:** Joško Gvardiol
- **Nickname:** "The Wall"
- **Position:** Centre-Back / Left-Back
- **Date of Birth:** January 23, 2002
- **Place of Birth:** Zagreb, Croatia
- **Height:** 1.85 m (6 ft 1 in)
- **Current Club:** Manchester City (Premier League)
- **Nationality:** Croatian

## Playing Style

Gvardiol is a modern ball-playing defender who combines defensive solidity with exceptional technical ability. Comfortable in possession and aggressive in challenges, he can play centre-back or left-back with equal effectiveness. His composure belies his young age.

## Key Skills
- **Ball Playing:** Calm and composed in possession, initiates attacks from the back
- **Defensive Positioning:** Reads the game excellently, rarely caught out
- **Passing Range:** Comfortable playing long diagonal passes
- **Versatility:** Equally effective at CB or LB
- **Aerial Ability:** Strong in the air at both ends of the pitch
