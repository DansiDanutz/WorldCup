# Dominik Livaković

## Profile
- **Full Name:** Dominik Livaković
- **Nickname:** "Domi"
- **Position:** Goalkeeper
- **Date of Birth:** January 9, 1995
- **Place of Birth:** Zadar, Croatia

## Playing Style

Livaković is a shot-stopping specialist whose heroics at the 2022 World Cup made him a global name. He combines excellent reflexes with commanding presence in the penalty area. His penalty-saving ability is among the best in world football.

## Key Skills
- **Shot Stopping:** Exceptional reflexes, particularly in one-on-one situations
- **Penalty Saving:** Saved 4 penalties at the 2022 World Cup alone
- **Commanding Presence:** Assertive in claiming crosses and organizing defense
- **Distribution:** Improved significantly with the ball at his feet
- **Big-Game Temperament:** Saves his best performances for the biggest moments
