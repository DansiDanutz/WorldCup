# Andrej Kramarić

## Profile
- **Full Name:** Andrej Kramarić
- **Nickname:** "Krama"
- **Position:** Striker / Second Striker
- **Date of Birth:** June 19, 1991
- **Place of Birth:** Zagreb, Croatia
- **Height:** 1.77 m (5 ft 10 in)
- **Current Club:** Hoffenheim (Bundesliga)
- **Nationality:** Croatian

## Playing Style

Kramarić is a technically gifted forward with exceptional finishing ability and intelligent movement. He excels at finding space in the penalty box and has one of the best conversion rates in Bundesliga history. A classic "fox in the box" with surprising creativity.

## Key Skills
- **Finishing:** Clinical with both feet, deadly from inside the box
- **Movement:** Intelligent runs that create separation from defenders
- **Link-Up Play:** Holds the ball well and brings others into play
- **Free Kicks:** Accurate set-piece specialist
- **Versatility:** Can play as a #9, #10, or on either wing
