# Tunisia vs Netherlands — Group F, Matchday 1

**Date:** June 12, 2026 | **Tournament:** FIFA World Cup 2026

---

## Head-to-Head History

Tunisia and the Netherlands have limited competitive history, having never met in a World Cup match previously.

---

## The Storyline: The Group Stage Curse

Tunisia has appeared in seven World Cups and never advanced beyond the group stage. In 2022, they came agonizingly close, beating France 1-0 but falling short on goal difference. Now they open against the Netherlands — a formidable challenge but also an opportunity.

Les Aigles de Carthage carry the spirit of 1978, when they became the first African team to win a World Cup match. That trailblazing legacy fuels every generation.

---

## Key Player Matchups

### Ellyes Skhiri vs Frenkie de Jong
Skhiri's defensive positioning against de Jong's midfield orchestration. If Skhiri disrupts the Dutch tempo, Tunisia can compete.

### Hannibal Mejbri vs Virgil van Dijk
Hannibal's creativity against van Dijk's commanding presence. The young playmaker must find a way through.

### Dylan Bronn vs Cody Gakpo
Bronn's defending against Gakpo's movement. Gakpo was the 2022 group-stage breakout star — Tunisia must contain him.

---

## Tactical Preview

**Tunisia (4-3-3):** Defensive discipline, compact midfield, counter-attacks. Physical and uncomfortable.

**Netherlands (3-4-2-1):** Patient build-up through de Jong. Gakpo and Memphis provide the cutting edge.

---

## X-Factor

**Tunisia's desperation to break the curse.** Seven World Cups, zero knockout appearances. If Tunisia scores first, belief could carry them to history.
