# Tunisia vs Japan — Group F, Matchday 2

**Date:** June 17, 2026 | **Tournament:** FIFA World Cup 2026

---

## Head-to-Head History

Tunisia and Japan have never met in a World Cup match.

---

## The Storyline: The Pivotal Match

With the Netherlands likely dominating Group F, second place could come down to this head-to-head. Both teams play organized, counter-attacking football — a fascinating tactical mirror match. Tunisia's physical presence against Japan's technical precision.

For Tunisia, a victory would be a massive step toward their first-ever knockout round. For Japan, continuing their remarkable World Cup consistency.

---

## Key Player Matchups

### Hannibal Mejbri vs Wataru Endo
Hannibal's creativity against Endo's ball-winning. The midfield battle determines tempo.

### Montassar Talbi vs Takefusa Kubo
Talbi's positioning against Kubo's dribbling brilliance. Kubo can beat anyone one-on-one.

### Elias Achouri vs Kaoru Mitoma
Two exciting wide players who can create from nothing.

---

## Tactical Preview

**Tunisia (4-3-3):** Compact and physical. Defensive discipline with counters through wide players. Set pieces are key.

**Japan (4-2-3-1):** Technical precision and rapid transitions. Kubo and Mitoma provide width and creativity.

---

## X-Factor

**The clash of styles.** Which team forces the other out of its comfort zone will prevail. The first 20 minutes reveal whose identity wins.
