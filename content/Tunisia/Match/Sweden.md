# Tunisia vs Sweden — Group F, Matchday 3

**Date:** June 22, 2026 | **Tournament:** FIFA World Cup 2026

---

## Head-to-Head History

Sweden and Tunisia have never met in a World Cup match.

---

## The Storyline: The Decider

Matchday 3 — the final group game. For both teams, this could determine whether they advance to the knockout rounds.

Sweden carries 13 World Cup appearances and a hunger to prove they belong after missing 2022. Tunisia carries a seven-World Cup curse — never advancing beyond the group stage. One of these stories changes today.

Sweden's physical, direct attacking against Tunisia's organized defense and counter-attacking threat. Both fight with desperation. The first goal is enormous.

---

## Key Player Matchups

### Viktor Gyokeres vs Montassar Talbi
Gyokeres' prolific finishing against Talbi's defensive discipline. Deny him space in the box.

### Alexander Isak vs Dylan Bronn
Isak's electrifying pace against Bronn's positioning. One through ball decides it.

### Hannibal Mejbri vs Victor Lindelof
Hannibal's creativity against Lindelof's experience. The Manchester United connection adds intrigue.

## Tactical Preview

**Sweden (4-3-3):** Direct attacking with Gyokeres. Set pieces are a major weapon. Physical superiority.

**Tunisia (4-3-3):** Organized defense, counter through Mejbri and wide players' pace.

**The knockout mentality.** The team that channels desperation into discipline — rather than recklessness — prevails.
