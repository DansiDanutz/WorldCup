## Squad Notes

- **Youth infusion:** Lamouchi made bold decisions in leaving out veterans Ferjani Sassi, Yassine Meriah, Ali Maaloul, and Naim Sliti (377 combined caps) in favor of younger players
- **European backbone:** 20 of 26 players are based in European leagues, giving Tunisia rare tactical sophistication for an African team
- **PSG teenager:** Khalil Ayari (19) is one of the youngest players at the tournament and represents an exciting future
- **Goalkeeping uncertainty:** Aymen Dahmen is the likely starter but has limited international experience compared to previous Tunisia World Cup goalkeepers
- **Creative burden:** Much of the attacking impetus falls on Hannibal Mejbri, who must deliver if Tunisia is to have any chance in a difficult group
