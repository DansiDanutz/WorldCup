### Skills and Strengths

Aaron Wan-Bissaka is widely regarded as one of the best one-on-one defenders in world football. His tackle success rate consistently ranks among the highest in Europe's top leagues.

**Key strengths:**
- **Tackling**: Elite-level ball-winner; his sliding tackles are signature moves
- **One-on-one defending**: Almost impossible to beat in isolated situations
- **Recovery pace**: Can chase down any forward from behind
- **Physicality**: Strong, athletic, and dominant in ground duels
- **Experience**: 200+ Premier League appearances
