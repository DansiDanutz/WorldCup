# Yoane Wissa

## Profile

- **Position:** Forward / Winger

- **Place of Birth:** Épinay-sur-Seine, France

## Playing Style

Wissa is a versatile forward whose pace, work rate, and finishing have made him a Premier League regular. He is DR Congo's most important attacking player, capable of playing anywhere across the front line.

## Key Skills
- **Pace:** Quick over short and long distances
- **Versatility:** Can play as a #9, left winger, or second striker
- **Finishing:** Clinical in front of goal
- **Work Rate:** Presses relentlessly from the front
- **Technical Ability:** Comfortable in possession
