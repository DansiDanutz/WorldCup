# Chancel Mbemba

## Profile
- **Full Name:** Chancel Mbemba Mangulu
- **Nickname:** "The Rock"
- **Position:** Centre-Back
- **Date of Birth:** August 8, 1994
- **Place of Birth:** Kinshasa, DR Congo
- **Height:** 1.82 m (6 ft 0 in)
- **Current Club:** Al-Wakrah (Qatar)
- **Nationality:** Congolese

## Playing Style

Mbemba is a powerful centre-back who combines physical dominance with surprising technical ability. A former Newcastle United player, he has matured into one of Africa's most reliable defenders.

## Key Skills
- **Physicality:** Strong in challenges and aerial duels
- **Pace:** Quick for a centre-back, can recover against fast forwards
- **Leadership:** Organizes the defense and leads by example
- **Aerial Ability:** Dominant at both ends from set pieces
- **Experience:** Premier League, Ligue 1, and international experience
