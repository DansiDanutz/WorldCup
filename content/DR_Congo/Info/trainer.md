# Sébastien Desabre — Head Coach

## Profile
- **Full Name:** Sébastien Desabre
- **Date of Birth:** August 2, 1976
- **Place of Birth:** Valence, France
- **Nationality:** French
- **Appointed:** 2022 (second stint)

## Coaching Career
Desabre is an Africa specialist who has managed across the continent:
- Uganda National Team
- ES Sahel (Tunisia)
- Wydad AC (Morocco)
- ASEC Mimosas (Ivory Coast)
- DR Congo National Team (2022-present)

## Tactical Philosophy
- **Defensive Organization:** Well-structured 4-2-3-1 system
- **Counter-Attacking:** Rapid transitions using pace of Wissa, Silas
- **Set Pieces:** Heavily drilled at both ends
- **Physicality:** Leverages the natural athleticism of the squad
- **Flexibility:** Adapts game plan to opposition
