# DR Congo vs Uzbekistan — Group K, Matchday 2

**Date:** June 17, 2026 | **Tournament:** FIFA World Cup 2026

---

## Head-to-Head History

First-ever meeting between DR Congo and Uzbekistan in official competition.

---

## The Storyline: The Battle of the Debut Dreams

Both teams are writing their World Cup stories — DR Congo ending a 52-year absence, Uzbekistan making their first-ever appearance. This match could determine which of these unlikely qualifiers advances to the knockout rounds.

The contrast is stark: DR Congo's physical power and European-based talent against Uzbekistan's disciplined organization and growing technical quality. Both teams will view this as winnable — and both will be desperate not to lose.

The stakes create a unique tension. A victory keeps knockout hopes alive; a defeat likely means elimination. Expect a cautious first half that explodes into life after the break.

---

## Key Player Matchups

### Cedric Bakambu vs Abdukodir Khusanov
Bakambu's predatory movement against Khusanov's positioning. The striker's experience could be the difference.

### Yoane Wissa vs Farrukh Sayfiev
Wissa's pace against Sayfiev's defensive discipline. DR Congo will look to release Wissa behind the Uzbekistan defense.

### Eldor Shomurodov vs Chancel Mbemba
Shomurodov's Serie A quality against Mbemba's defensive leadership. Both know European football — this will be a tactical chess match.

## Tactical Preview

**DR Congo (4-3-3):** More proactive than against Portugal. DR Congo can express their attacking quality while maintaining defensive discipline. Set pieces remain a major weapon.

**Uzbekistan (4-2-3-1):** Organized and disciplined, looking to frustrate DR Congo and strike through Shomurodov's quality. The White Wolves will be compact and hard to break down.

---

## X-Factor

**Set pieces.** Two physically matched teams with strong aerial presence — dead-ball situations could be the decisive factor. One corner, one free kick, one header could send either team through.
