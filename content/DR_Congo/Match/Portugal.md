# DR Congo vs Portugal — Group K, Matchday 1

**Date:** June 12, 2026 | **Tournament:** FIFA World Cup 2026

---

## Head-to-Head History

First-ever meeting between DR Congo and Portugal in official competition.

---

## The Storyline: 52 Years of Waiting

DR Congo waited 52 years between World Cup appearances. Their only previous appearance was in 1974, when they became the first sub-Saharan African team to qualify. Now, generations later, the Leopards return — and their opening opponent is one of the tournament favorites.

For DR Congo's European-based players, this is personal. Aaron Wan-Bissaka, rejected by England's youth setup, chose to represent his Congolese heritage. Arthur Masuaku, Yoane Wissa, Cedric Bakambu — they all know Portuguese football from their club careers. They won't be overawed.

The question is whether DR Congo's physical power and emotional intensity can overcome Portugal's technical superiority. The Leopards have nothing to lose — and that makes them dangerous.

---

## Key Player Matchups

### Cedric Bakambu vs Ruben Dias
Bakambu's movement and finishing against Dias' defensive class. If Bakambu gets a chance, he has the quality to take it.

### Aaron Wan-Bissaka vs Rafael Leao
Wan-Bissaka's elite one-on-one defending against Leao's explosive dribbling. This is a Premier League-quality matchup.

### Yoane Wissa vs Joao Cancelo
Wissa's pace on the counter against Cancelo's attacking instincts. The space behind Cancelo could be DR Congo's highway to goal.

---

## Tactical Preview

**DR Congo (4-3-3):** Sit deep, defend with discipline, and explode on the counter. The pace of Wissa and Silas Katompa can trouble any defense. Set pieces are a major weapon with the team's physical presence.

**Portugal (3-4-2-1):** Patient build-up, looking to stretch DR Congo's defensive block. Ronaldo will be the target in the box. Bernardo Silva and Bruno Fernandes will probe for openings.

---

## X-Factor

**The emotion of the return.** A 52-year wait creates an energy that cannot be measured. If DR Congo channels that emotion into disciplined performance rather than reckless abandon, they could shock the world.
