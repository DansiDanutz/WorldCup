## The Storyline: African Power vs South American Flair

This cross-continental clash pits DR Congo's physical, counter-attacking style against Colombia's technical brilliance and attacking verve. It's a matchup that could produce spectacular football — or a tense, tactical stalemate.

For DR Congo, this represents a chance to test themselves against one of South America's finest. For Colombia, it's about securing advancement without taking unnecessary risks.

Luis Diaz against Aaron Wan-Bissaka is a Premier League-quality matchup that could define the match. Diaz's electrifying pace against Wan-Bissaka's world-class one-on-one defending — this battle alone is worth the price of admission.
