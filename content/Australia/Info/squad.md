## Squad Notes

- **New Faces:** Cristian Volpato's inclusion is the headline story -- the Italy-born playmaker switched allegiance to Australia just days before the squad announcement
- **Experience:** Mathew Ryan (104 caps), Mathew Leckie (82 caps), and Jackson Irvine (81 caps) provide the backbone
- **A-League contingent:** Five domestic-based players (Beach, Behich, Leckie, Velupillay, Okon Jr)
- **Notable absentees:** Several 2022 World Cup veterans were not selected as Popovic has deliberately refreshed the squad
- **Goal threat from defense:** Harry Souttar's 11 international goals from center-back is a remarkable strike rate
