## Skills and Strengths

- **Physical dominance**: At 1.90 m with explosive power, he overwhelms defenders with his size and strength
- **Goal-scoring record**: Belgium's all-time top scorer with 89+ goals -- a remarkable record of consistency
- **Left foot**: Devastating left-footed finisher with power and accuracy
- **Counter-attacking threat**: At his best when running at defenders at full speed with space behind
- **Hold-up play**: Can receive the ball under pressure and bring teammates into play
- **Aerial ability**: Strong in the air at both ends of the pitch
- **Big-game scorer**: Has scored crucial goals in World Cups and European Championships
- **Experience**: Has played in the Premier League, Serie A, and across multiple major tournaments

## Interesting Facts

1. **He is Belgium's all-time top scorer by a massive margin**: Lukaku surpassed Bernard Voorhoof's record of 30 goals (which had stood since 1940) and has since pushed the record to 89+ goals. He is the second-highest European men's international goalscorer of all time, behind only Cristiano Ronaldo. His scoring rate for Belgium (roughly 0.74 goals per game) is remarkably consistent across three different decades and multiple tournaments.

2. **He is the cousin of NBA player Thanasis Antetokounmpo**: Lukaku's family has deep sporting connections. He is of Congolese descent through his father, Roger Lukaku, who was also a professional footballer. The family connection to the Antetokounmpo family (Giannis and Thanasis of NBA fame) comes through shared Congolese-Belgian heritage. Lukaku has spoken about growing up in poverty in Antwerp and how football was his way out, driving the relentless determination that has defined his career.
