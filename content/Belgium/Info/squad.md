## Squad Notes

- **Captain**: Youri Tielemans -- the Aston Villa midfielder was named captain by Rudi Garcia
- **All-time top scorer**: Romelu Lukaku holds Belgium's scoring record with 89+ goals, second all-time among European men's international goalscorers
- **Most capped player**: Axel Witsel (37 years old) has 130+ caps and can play midfield or defence
- **Premier League/Aston Villa pair**: Tielemans and Onana provide midfield chemistry from their time together at Aston Villa
- **Serie A influence**: Lukaku (Napoli), De Winter (AC Milan), Saelemaekers (AC Milan), De Ketelaere (Atalanta)
- **New generation**: Fernandez-Pardo (20), Penders (20), Debast (21), Moreira (21) represent Belgium's future
- **Defensive depth**: 9 defenders in the squad, reflecting Garcia's emphasis on defensive options
- **Key absence**: No major injury absences reported in the final 26
- **Transition era**: The squad blends the remaining "Golden Generation" (De Bruyne, Courtois, Lukaku, Witsel, Meunier) with emerging talent
- **FIFA vs Olympics classification**: Moreira and Saelemaekers are listed as midfielders by FIFA but as forwards by Olympics.com -- same 26 players regardless
