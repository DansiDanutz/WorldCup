## Interesting Facts

1. **He became Belgium's youngest-ever senior goalkeeper at age 19**: Courtois made his senior international debut for Belgium in 2011 at just 19 years old, becoming the youngest goalkeeper ever to represent the country at senior level. This was remarkable not just because of his age, but because he was already playing for Atletico Madrid on loan from Chelsea, having barely featured for his parent club. The Belgian federation took the bold decision to make him their number one, and he has repaid that faith with over 100 caps and more than a decade of world-class service.

2. **His 2022 Champions League final performance is considered one of the greatest ever**: In the 2022 Champions League final against Liverpool, Courtois made 9 saves -- the most by any goalkeeper in a Champions League final since 2003-04. He repeatedly denied Mohamed Salah, Sadio Mane, and the Liverpool attack in a 1-0 Real Madrid victory. After the match, he said: "Today nobody was going to take that trophy from me." The performance was so dominant that it silenced critics who had questioned whether he was truly among the all-time great goalkeepers.
