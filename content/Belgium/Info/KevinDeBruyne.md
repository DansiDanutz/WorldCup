## Skills and Strengths

- **Passing range**: Arguably the best passer in world football -- can deliver any type of pass with precision
- **Vision**: Sees passes and angles that no one else on the pitch can see
- **Assist machine**: Holds the record for most assists in Premier League history, consistently creating goals for teammates
- **Long-range shooting**: Capable of spectacular goals from distance with both feet
- **Set-piece delivery**: Elite at free kicks and corners
- **Work rate**: Combines creative genius with relentless running and pressing
- **Two-footed**: Equally effective with left and right foot
- **Leadership**: A vocal and demanding presence who drives standards in training and matches

## Interesting Facts

1. **Chelsea discarded him after just 9 appearances**: Jose Mourinho sold De Bruyne to Wolfsburg in January 2014 for just 18 million pounds, claiming he was not good enough for Chelsea's first team. De Bruyne had made only 9 appearances for the club and was frustrated by a lack of opportunities. Within 18 months of leaving Chelsea, he was the Bundesliga's best player. Within two years, he was back in the Premier League at Manchester City for 55 million pounds. He has since become arguably the greatest midfielder in Premier League history -- making Mourinho's decision one of the most famously mistaken transfers in football.

2. **He is surprisingly candid about his temper on the pitch**: In a revealing Players' Tribune article, De Bruyne admitted: "It's true that I can blow up sometimes, especially on the pitch. I tend to hold things inside, and then bang -- I lose my head. Usually five minutes later, I'm fine again." His intense competitiveness sometimes boils over into visible frustration, but those closest to him say it comes from a place of caring deeply about winning. He is also known for his thoughtful, introspective personality off the pitch, where he is more philosopher than firebrand.
