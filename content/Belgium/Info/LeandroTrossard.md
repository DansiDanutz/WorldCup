## Interesting Facts

1. **He made his Premier League debut at 24 and became one of its most consistent scorers**: Unlike many top players who break through as teenagers, Trossard had a late start in elite football. He spent his early career in Belgium, including a loan spell at OH Leuven in the second division, and did not move to the Premier League until he was 24. Even at Brighton, he was initially considered a squad player before blossoming into one of the league's most reliable attackers. His rise from Belgian football's lower levels to starting for Arsenal and Belgium is a testament to perseverance and continuous improvement.

2. **He once scored a hat-trick against Manchester City at the Etihad**: On October 31, 2021, while playing for Brighton, Trossard scored a stunning hat-trick in a dramatic 4-1 loss at the Etihad Stadium. Despite the defeat, his individual performance was extraordinary -- he became one of the very few players to score three goals away at Manchester City under Pep Guardiola. The performance helped convince Arsenal to sign him, and he has since become one of their most reliable attackers, scoring crucial goals in title races and European competitions.
