# 72-Hour Campaign Calendar

Start when the final video is available or when the first post goes live.

## Day 1: Launch The Invite

### Hours 0-3

- Publish the main video on all owned short-form channels.
- Send the personal WhatsApp/Telegram invite to warm contacts.
- Post Feed 1 from `copy-bank.md`.
- Lead with the free-picks flow: save 3 teams first, paid leaderboard only with ticket.
- Log every post in `posting-log-template.csv`.

### Hours 4-8

- Post one story/status with the referral code large and clear.
- Share one still image or screen recording of the Pick 3 Teams flow.
- Mention the private points preview, not only the paid leaderboard.
- Reply to all questions within 30 minutes.

### Hours 9-16

- Post the risk/reward angle: favorites vs underdogs.
- Send the group invite only to groups where football/game posts are welcome.
- Ask first joiners to reply with their 3 picks.

### Hours 17-24

- Repost the video with a different caption.
- Publish a short text post: `What 3 teams would you pick?`
- Review log: channels posted, replies, joins, referral code issues.

## Day 2: Social Proof And Conversation

### Hours 24-32

- Post a screenshot or clip from the picks preview / leaderboard context.
- Ask a debate question: favorites, underdogs, surprise team.
- Push direct invite to friends who reacted but did not join.

### Hours 33-40

- Post Feed 2: friends angle.
- Publish a WhatsApp status with the link.
- Reply bank focus: explain simple rules.

### Hours 41-48

- Post video again with Caption 3.
- Share referral/agent angle with people who can bring groups.
- Check the link works from mobile.

## Day 3: Conversion Push

### Hours 49-56

- Post the direct CTA: `Join with code 26BC4B90CB`.
- Share a short step list: sign in, code, pick 3 teams free, use ticket later for paid leaderboard.
- Ask existing users to send the link to 3 friends.

### Hours 57-64

- Post short-form video again on channels where repeat posting is allowed.
- Post Feed 3: favorites vs underdogs.
- Continue comment replies.

### Hours 65-72

- Final 72-hour push: `The tournament has not started yet, all teams still available.`
- Share best comments/questions as follow-up posts.
- Export the posting log and summarize what worked.
