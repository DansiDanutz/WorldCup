## How to price it (don't guess — anchor it)
You have no revenue yet, so price on **cost-to-recreate + time-to-market**, not a revenue multiple:
- **Build-cost anchor:** estimate the dev hours to rebuild (auth, wallet, admin, scoring, referral, compliance, marketing kit) × a realistic rate. That's your floor.
- **Time-to-market premium:** "live and ready for THIS World Cup" is worth a premium *only this week* — lean on the urgency.
- **Suggested structure:** offer 3 options — (1) **license/white-label** for a flat fee + small rev-share, (2) **outright sale** of the asset, (3) **rev-share pilot** (lowest friction; gets a yes fast).
- Put a number in the room first (anchoring). A wide range is fine; "from $X" invites the conversation.
