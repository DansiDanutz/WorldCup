# Influencer Outreach — Email + DM templates

> Audience: football / fantasy-football / betting-tipster creators on YouTube,
> TikTok, Instagram, X, and Telegram. The pitch is your **agent/referral deal**
> (they bring the audience, they earn % — no cost to them or their followers).
>
> Personalize the {{tokens}}. Generic blasts get ignored; one specific line
> about *their* content lifts reply rates more than anything else.

2. {{first_name}} — World Cup Pick-3 game + revenue share before kickoff

## Personalization checklist (do these before sending)
- [ ] Real first name (not "there")
- [ ] One specific, true line about their content
- [ ] Right platform named
- [ ] Their niche (fantasy / tipster / club fan / highlights)

## Sending rules so you don't get spam-foldered
- Use a **separate sending domain** (e.g., `worldcup26.team` or `getworldcup26.com`), not your main domain.
- Set up **SPF + DKIM + DMARC** before the first send.
- Ramp slowly: **20–40/day per inbox**, not 500 at once. Use 2–3 inboxes.
- Verify every address (NeverBounce / ZeroBounce) — bounces kill deliverability.
- Tooling for sequencing + tracking: Instantly, Smartlead, or Lemlist.
- For creators, **DMs usually beat email**. Prioritize DMs for <100k accounts.

## Compliance
- This is a real-money prize product. In any post the creator makes, ask them to
  include **18+** and disclose the partnership (#ad / "partner") per FTC/ASA rules.
